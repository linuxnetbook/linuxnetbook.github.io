ASUS Eee PC 1005HA

Brand: ASUS
Operating System: Xandros
Display: 10.1" Backlit LED (1024x600)
Dimensions (WxDxH): 10.2" x 6.9" x 1.4"
Weight: 2.8 lbs
Processor: 1.6 GHz Intel Atom N270
Memory: 1 GB DDR2 (max: 2 GB)
Storage: 160 GB HDD
Battery life: 10.5 hours
WLAN: WiFi 802.11b/g/n
LAN: 10/100 Ethernet
Colors: Crystal Black, Royal Blue
Input Devices: Keyboard, Touchpad, Microphone, Video Camera
Connectivity: Bluetooth, USB, SD Card reader

The ASUS Eee PC 1005HA, a.k.a Seashell, is a 10.1" netbook powered by an 1.6 GHz Intel Atom N270 Processor. It is equipped with 1 GB DDR2 RAM, a 160 GB hard drive, a 1.3-megapixel webcam, and offers easy mobile connectivity via WiFi 802.11b/g/n and Bluetooth v2.1.

The Linux distribution is presumably Xandros as with other Linux based models from the Eee PC series, which is based on Debian and features applications for music and photo management, CD and DVD authoring tools, Web browser, clients for instant messaging, and e-mail, an office suite, and many more.
