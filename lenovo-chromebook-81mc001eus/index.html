Lenovo 500e Chromebook 81MC001EUS Touchscreen Laptop

Brand: Lenovo
Operating System: Chrome OS
Display: 11.6" HD (1366x768)
Dimensions (WxDxH): 11.4" x 8.0" x 0.8"
Touch Screen: Yes
Weight: 2.89 lbs
Processor: 1.1 GHz Intel Celeron N4120
Memory: 4 GB LPDDR4
Storage: 32 GB Flash Memory
WLAN: Wi-Fi 802.11ac
Colors: Black
Input Devices: Keyboard, Touchpad, Touchscreen, Webcam
Connectivity: Bluetooth, HDMI, USB, Wi-Fi

The Lenovo 500e Chromebook 2nd Gen (model 81MC001EUS) is a lightweight, mid-range Chromebook tailored for education and casual users. With its 2-in-1 convertible design and modest hardware, it strikes a balance between functionality and affordability.

Key Features:

  • Display: 11.6" HD touchscreen with a resolution of 1366 x 768. It offers decent image clarity for multimedia tasks and supports touch input for ease of use in tablet mode.
  • Processor: Powered by an Intel Celeron N4120 quad-core processor with a base clock rate of 1.1 GHz, suitable for running Chrome OS and handling basic daily workflows.
  • Memory and Storage: Equipped with 4 GB of LPDDR4 RAM and 32 GB of flash storage, this laptop is ideal for web browsing, document editing, and cloud-based applications.
  • Input Devices: Includes a keyboard, a touchpad, a webcam, and a touchscreen—making it versatile both as a laptop and tablet alternative.
  • Connectivity: Comes with Wi-Fi 802.11ac, Bluetooth, USB ports, and an HDMI port to ensure compatibility with peripherals and external displays.
  • Build and Design: Weighing 2.89 pounds and measuring 11.4" wide, 8" deep, and 0.8" thick, the device is lightweight and portable, ideal for students and on-the-go professionals.
  • Operating System: Runs Chrome OS, offering seamless access to Google services and Play Store apps.

Who Is It For?

The Lenovo 500e Chromebook 2nd Gen is well-suited for educational purposes, light productivity, or entertainment tasks. Its sturdy build and ease of use make it ideal for school environments or users seeking a reliable secondary device for their basic computing needs.

Limitations

While functional, the 500e Chromebook is not designed for heavy workloads or intensive applications. The availability of only 32 GB of storage suggests reliance on cloud-based solutions or external storage for more demanding tasks. Additionally, its HD resolution may feel limited for users accustomed to Full HD or higher displays.

Summary

With user-friendly features, a durable build, and a budget-conscious approach, the Lenovo 500e Chromebook 2nd Gen offers an accessible, efficient option for basic computing—and particularly shines in educational settings.
