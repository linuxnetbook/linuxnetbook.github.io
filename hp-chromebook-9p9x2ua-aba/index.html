HP Chromebook Plus 14a-nf0050nr

Brand: HP
Operating System: Chrome OS
Display: 14" FHD IPS (1920x1080)
Dimensions (WxDxH): 12.82" x 8.48" x 0.72"
Weight: 3.2 lbs
Processor: 3.8 GHz Intel Core i3-N305
Memory: 8 GB LPDDR4X
Storage: 128 GB UFS
Battery life: 11 hours
WLAN: Wi-Fi 6
Colors: Meteor silver cover and keyboard frame, mineral silver base
Input Devices: Keyboard, Touchpad, Webcam, HP True Vision FHD camera (with privacy shutter)
Connectivity: Bluetooth 5.3, HDMI, USB-C, USB-A, Wi-Fi 6

The HP Chromebook Plus 14 is a versatile laptop designed to balance productivity and entertainment. With its Intel Core i3-N305 processor, 8 GB of RAM, and 128 GB of UFS storage, it provides ample power for multitasking and enough storage space for your files while maintaining smooth operation. Powered by Chrome OS, it's updated automatically, protected against viruses, and equipped with access to a variety of apps.

Key Features:

  • Sturdy Performance: The Intel Core i3-N305, an 8-core processor with a clock rate up to 3.8 GHz, delivers the power you need for an efficient workflow—whether you're working, learning, or streaming.
  • Ample Multitasking Memory: 8 GB of LPDDR4X RAM ensures that multitasking is fast and seamless.
  • Vivid Visuals: The 14-inch FHD IPS display offers a 1920x1080 resolution with 178-degree wide-viewing angles for vibrant, crystal-clear images.
  • Storage for Your Needs: The 128 GB UFS storage is fast, reliable, and power-efficient, providing enough space for your files, photos, and apps.
  • Webcam and Privacy: The HP True Vision FHD camera with a built-in privacy shutter ensures clear video conferencing while maintaining your privacy when not in use.

Designed for Connectivity and Ease

With built-in Wi-Fi 6 and Bluetooth 5.3, expect faster speeds and reliable connections for streaming, downloads, or video calls. The addition of USB-A, USB-C, and HDMI ports makes the device versatile and ideal for users requiring diverse connectivity options.

Environmentally Friendly Design

The HP Chromebook Plus 14 is EPEAT Gold certified with Climate+ registration and ENERGY STAR-rated. Built with sustainability in mind, it is made from post-consumer recycled plastics.

Ideal For On-the-Go Work and Play

Weighing only 3.2 pounds and measuring just 0.72 inches thick, this Chromebook is lightweight and compact, making it easy to carry. With a sleek metallic "meteor silver" finish, it offers a premium style while staying affordable.

For an affordable device with robust features, the HP Chromebook Plus 14 fits the bill for productivity, entertainment, and sustainability-conscious users.
