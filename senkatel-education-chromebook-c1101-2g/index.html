Senkatel Education Chromebook C1101-2G

Brand: Senkatel
Operating System: Chrome OS
Display: 11.6" Anti-Glare IPS (1366x768)
Dimensions (WxDxH): 11.8" x 8.5" x 0.9"
Weight: 2.9 lbs
Processor: 2.16 GHz Intel Celeron N2830
Memory: 2 GB DDR3 SDRAM
Storage: 16 GB SSD
Battery life: 9.5 hours
WLAN: Wi-Fi 802.11ac
Colors: White/Graphite
Input Devices: Keyboard, Touchpad, Microphone, Video Camera
Connectivity: USB, SD Card reader, Bluetooth

Senkatel is a Toronto based company, that specializes in affordable devices and software solutions for education and enterprise markets.

Being targeted at students the Senkatel Education Chromebook C1101-2G has some features that set it apart from most other Chromebooks:

  • water-resistant, ergonomic keyboard with anti-peel keys
  • a touchpad that channels liquids away from the electronics to prevent damage
  • ruggedized design capable of withstanding bumps and drops up to 70cm
  • integrated carry handle

The Senkatel C1101-2G has a 11.6-inch anti-glare LCD screen with In-Plane Switching (IPS), is powered by a 2.16 GHz dual-core Intel Celeron N2830 processor, and comes with 2 GB RAM, a 16GB solid state drive, HDMI port, 1 x USB 3.0 and 1 x USB 2.0 ports, a Kensington security lock slot and a 3-cell battery that offers up to 9.5 hours of usage.

The Senkatel Education Chromebook C1101-2G was offered for an initial price of US $313.99. For current offers in the US, check out this product page on Amazon.com. You can get cheaper Chromebooks with similar performance, but the extra care that went into making the C1101-2G more durable may be worth a higher price.
