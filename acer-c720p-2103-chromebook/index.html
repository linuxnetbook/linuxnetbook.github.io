Acer C720P-2103 Chromebook

Brand: Acer
Operating System: Chrome OS
Display: 11.6" Active Matrix TFT Color LCD (1366x768)
Dimensions (WxDxH): 11.34" x 8.03" x 0.75"
Weight: 2.76 lbs
Processor: 1.4 GHz Intel Celeron 2955U
Memory: 2 GB DDR3L SDRAM
Storage: 16 GB SSD
Battery life: 8.5 hours
WLAN: Wi-Fi 802.11 a/b/g/n
Colors: Granite Gray
Input Devices: Keyboard, Touchpad, Microphone, Video Camera
Connectivity: USB, SD Card reader, Bluetooth

The Acer C720P-2103 Chromebook has an 11.6-inch screen and is powered by a dual-core Intel Celeron 2955U processor. The device is equipped with 2GB RAM, 16 GB SSD storage, 1x USB 3.0, 1x USB 2.0, 1x HDMI™ ports, Bluetooth 4.0 and Wi-Fi 802.11 a/b/g/n.

Being a small and lightweight laptop at the lower end of the performance spectrum, the Acer C720P-2103 is suitable for mobile communication and consumption of content and media. To get serious work done, you should look for a higher end Chromebook.

The Acer C720P-2103 was released in January 2016 for an initial price of US $179.99.
