H6 AllWinner A10

Brand: Unknown
Operating System: Android
Display: 10" LCD (1024x600)
Dimensions (WxDxH): 10.9" x 7.4" x 0.9"
Weight: 1.78 lbs
Processor: 1.0 GHz AllWinner A10
Memory: 512 MB
Storage: 4 GB SSD
Battery life: 3 hours
WLAN: WiFi 802.11 b/g/n
LAN: 10/100 Ethernet
Colors: Black, White
Input Devices: Keyboard, Touchpad, Video Camera
Connectivity: USB, SD Card reader

The H6 is a low-cost 10" netbook from China powered by a 1.0GHz AllWinner A10 processor, a Mali 400 GPU and 512MB RAM, it comes pre-installed with Android 4.0.3. The lighweight device supports common video and audio formats, dozens of languages ranging from Afrikaans to Traditional Chinese and has a standard 80 keys English keyboard.

Pre-installed applications include browser, calendar, file manager, Gmail, and Skype. More applications can be installed via the Android market. The netbook likely also runs other Linuxes, but with a 1024x600 screen resolution you may experience problems such as non-visible user interface elements.

The H6 is currently being offered on pandawill.com for $140.99 including worldwide shipping, which seems like a good price looking at the hardware specs, but is somehow spoiled by only 3 hours of battery life.
