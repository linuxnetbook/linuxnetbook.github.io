Lenovo Chromebook Duet 83HH0014US Convertible Touchscreen Laptop

Brand: Lenovo
Operating System: Chrome OS
Display: 10.95" WUXGA IPS (1920x1200)
Dimensions (WxDxH): 10.95" x 10.05" x 0.29"
Touch Screen: Yes
Weight: 1.12 lbs
Processor: 2.6 GHz MediaTek Kompanio 838
Memory: 4 GB LPDDR4X
Storage: 64GB GB eMMC
Battery life: 12 hours
WLAN: 802.11ac
Colors: Luna Gray
Input Devices: Keyboard, Touchpad, Webcam
Connectivity: Bluetooth, HDMI, USB Type-C, Wi-Fi

The Lenovo Chromebook Duet 2024 edition is a lightweight, 2-in-1 convertible device that combines portability with productivity. Designed for users who are always on the go, this Chromebook offers a versatile, durable build and robust features to handle daily tasks.

Key Features

Compact and Durable Design

Encased in a full-metal chassis with Corning glass protection and a soft folio stand, the Chromebook Duet is built to last. Its slim profile (0.29 inches thick) and lightweight body (1.122 lbs) make it highly portable, perfect for life on the move.

Display and Audio

The device features a 10.95-inch WUXGA IPS touch display with narrow bezels and a resolution of 1920x1200, delivering an immersive, cinema-like experience. For audio, it comes equipped with SmartAMP via Waves MaxxAudio, providing rich and immersive stereo sound for streaming and entertainment.

Performance

Powered by the MediaTek Kompanio 838 processor, the Chromebook Duet ensures snappy performance and efficient on-device AI processing. With 4GB of LPDDR4X RAM and 64GB eMMC storage, it's well-suited for light multitasking and cloud-based workflows. ChromeOS adds seamless integration with Google services, including fast boot times under 10 seconds.

Connectivity and Input

It offers a range of connectivity options that include USB Type-C ports, Wi-Fi (802.11ac), Bluetooth, and HDMI support. Input options include a detachable folio keyboard, a touchpad, and a built-in webcam with a physical shutter for privacy.

Camera

The front camera features advanced image processing for video calls and photography, even in low-light conditions, ensuring clear visuals while maintaining privacy with the physical shutter.

Final Thoughts

The Lenovo Chromebook Duet (2024) is a well-rounded device for users seeking simplicity, portability, and versatility. Whether you're a student, professional, or casual user, its convertible design, strong build, and efficient specs make it a practical choice.
