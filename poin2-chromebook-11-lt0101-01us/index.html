Poin2 Chromebook 11 LT0101 01US

Brand: Poin2
Operating System: Chrome OS
Display: 11.6" LED Backlight HD Display (1366x768)
Dimensions (WxDxH): 11.4" x 8.03" x 0.76"
Weight: 2.5 lbs
Processor: 1.8 GHz Rockchip RK3288
Memory: 2 GB DDR3L SDRAM
Storage: 16 GB eMMC
Battery life: 8.5 hours
WLAN: Wi-Fi 802.11 a/c
Colors: Black
Input Devices: Keyboard, Touchpad, Microphone, Video Camera
Connectivity: USB, SD Card reader

The Poin2 Chromebook 11 is a very cheap laptop with a 11.6-inch screen powered by a quad-core Rockchip RK3288 Coretex-A17 processor clocked at 1.8 GHz. It comes with 2 GB RAM, 16 GB eMMC storage, 1 x HDMI and 2 x USB 2.0 ports, Micro SD card slot, Wireless LAN a/b/g/n/ac, Bluetooth 4.0 and offers up to 8.5 hours of battery life.

The Poin2 Chromebook has the same specs and low initial price of $149 as the Hisense Chromebook, making it one of the cheapest Chromebooks available to date. These different cheap models with the same specs were most probably assembled in the same Chinese factories.
