Lenovo Chromebook 81ER0002US

Brand: Lenovo
Operating System: Chrome OS
Display: 11.6" LED backlight (1366x768)
Dimensions (WxDxH): 11.4" x 8.0" x 0.8"
Weight: 2.76 lbs
Processor: 1.1 GHz Intel Celeron N3350
Memory: 4 GB LPDDR4 SDRAM (max: 4 GB)
Storage: 32 GB eMMC
Battery life: 10 hours
WLAN: 802.11a/b/g/n/ac
Colors: Black
Input Devices: Keyboard, Touchpad, Webcam
Connectivity: Bluetooth, HDMI, USB, Wi-Fi

The Lenovo 11.6-inch Chromebook, model 81ER0002US, is a practical option for students, professionals, and casual users seeking a simple and efficient computing solution. With a dual-core Intel Celeron N3350 processor clocked at 1.1 GHz and a max turbo speed of 2.4 GHz, this Chromebook is designed to handle essential tasks with ease. It features 4 GB of soldered LPDDR4 SDRAM, operating at 1600 MHz, ensuring smooth multitasking capabilities, while the 32 GB eMMC SSD provides adequate storage for cloud-centric operations.

The device sports a compact 11.6-inch LED backlit display with a resolution of 1366 x 768, making it suitable for browsing, streaming, and document editing. Its display boasts a 16:9 aspect ratio and 250 cd/m² brightness. The integrated Intel HD Graphics 500 enhances visual output without excessive power consumption.

Connectivity is well-covered with Bluetooth 4.2, 802.11a/b/g/n/ac Wi-Fi, multiple USB ports, plus HDMI output. The device also includes a microSD card reader for expandable storage. For audio and video needs, it comes equipped with stereo speakers and a 720p webcam.

Constructed from polycarbonate ABS, the Chromebook is light, weighing 2.76 lbs, and measures 11.4 x 8 x 0.8 inches, making it extremely portable. Battery life extends up to 10 hours on a single charge, powering through a typical workday without needing a recharge.

Security features, such as an administrator password and power-on password, enhance device protection, while a security lock slot offers peace of mind. Rounding out the package is an included power adapter and the standard compliance with RoHS standards.
