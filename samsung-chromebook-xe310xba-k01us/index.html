Samsung Chromebook 4 XE310XBA-K01US - 11.6-inch Laptop

Brand: Samsung
Operating System: Chrome OS
Display: 11.6" HD (1366x768)
Dimensions (WxDxH): 11.33" x 7.96" x 0.66"
Weight: 3.8 lbs
Processor: 1.1 GHz Intel Celeron N4000
Memory: 4 GB DDR4
Storage: 32GB GB eMMC
Battery life: 12.5 hours
WLAN: Gigabit Wi-Fi
Colors: Platinum Titan
Input Devices: Keyboard, Touchpad, Webcam
Connectivity: Bluetooth, HDMI, USB, Wi-Fi, Gigabit Wi-Fi

The Samsung Chromebook 4 is a lightweight, compact device aimed at users seeking portability and affordability without compromising on essential features. With a sleek 11.6" HD display housed in a durable Platinum Titan chassis, it's an appealing notebook for students, travelers, and light productivity users.

Key Features:

  • Portability: Weighing just 3.8 pounds and offering a slim profile (0.66" height, 11.33" width, 7.96" depth), it's designed for easy mobility.
  • Performance: Powered by an Intel Celeron N4000 processor, paired with 4GB of DDR4 RAM and 32GB of eMMC storage, it's well-suited for web browsing, streaming, and cloud-based applications.
  • Advanced Connectivity: The Chromebook supports Bluetooth, USB, HDMI, and features ultra-fast Gigabit Wi-Fi for seamless online experiences.
  • Exceptional Battery Life: With up to 12.5 hours of battery life, it can last through an entire day of use without needing a charge.
  • Built-in Security: Chrome OS offers multiple layers of built-in virus protection, ensuring secure operation out of the box.
  • Google Assistant Enabled: Built-in voice control provides added convenience for multitasking and executing commands hands-free.

Durability & Design

Built to military-grade standards, the Samsung Chromebook 4 can withstand accidental bumps and drops, making it a reliable choice for daily use in rugged environments. Its simple yet elegant design in "Platinum Titan" gives it a professional appearance.

Summary

Released on October 7, 2019, the Samsung Chromebook 4 strikes a balance between affordability and usability. While its hardware is modest, it has enough power to handle day-to-day tasks efficiently. Thanks to Google Assistant integration and Chrome OS, it also offers a modern user experience. If you're in the market for a budget-friendly device that prioritizes portability, durability, and connectivity, this Chromebook is worth considering.
