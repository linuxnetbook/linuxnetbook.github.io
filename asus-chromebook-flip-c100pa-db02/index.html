ASUS Chromebook Flip C100PA-DB02

Brand: ASUS
Operating System: Chrome OS
Display: 10.1" Multi-touch IPS display (1280x800)
Dimensions (WxDxH): 10.6" x 7.2" x 0.6"
Touch Screen: Yes
Weight: 2 lbs
Processor: 1.8 GHz Rockchip 3288-C
Memory: 4 GB DDR3
Storage: 16 GB eMMC
Battery life: 9 hours
WLAN: Wi-Fi 802.11ac
Colors: Silver
Input Devices: Keyboard, Touchpad, Microphone, Video Camera
Connectivity: Bluetooth, USB, SD Card reader

The ASUS Flip C100PA-DB02 is a convertible Chromebook with an IPS touchscreen that can be flipped over from 0 to 360 degree so the device can be used as a tablet or laptop.

The 10.1" screen device with an aluminum chassis is one of the lightest Chromebooks on the market. It offers sufficient usage time with up to 9 hours, making it a very attractive for mobile use cases.

This Chromebook is powered by a 1.8 GHz quad-core Rockchip 3288-C processor and equipped with 16 GB eMMC, 4 GB RAM, WiFi 802.11 a/c, an HD Webcam, 2 x USB 2.0 and 1 x micro HDMI ports, a MicroSD card reader and Bluetooth.

The ASUS Flip C100PA-DB02 was offered for an initial price of $279.00, which is pretty competitive for its specs.
