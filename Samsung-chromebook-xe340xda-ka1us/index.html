Samsung Galaxy Chromebook Go - 14-inch Chrome Laptop

Brand: Samsung
Operating System: Chrome OS
Display: 14" (1920x1080)
Dimensions (WxDxH): 12.88" x 8.88" x 0.63"
Touch Screen: Yes
Weight: 3.2 lbs
Processor: 2.8 GHz Intel Celeron N4500
Memory: 4 GB None
Storage: 32 GB eMMC
Battery life: 12 hours
WLAN: Wi-Fi 6
Colors: Silver
Input Devices: Keyboard, Touchpad, Webcam
Connectivity: Bluetooth, HDMI, USB, Wi-Fi

Samsung's Galaxy Chromebook Go stands as an appealing option for those in need of a lightweight, durable device that prioritizes portability and efficiency. Released on July 15, 2021, this Chromebook is designed with the modern user in mind, offering a balance of affordability, features, and reliability.

Build and Design

At only 3.2 lbs in weight and with a slim design (0.63" thick), the Chromebook Go is highly portable, ideal for students or professionals on the go. Its silver finish lends it a clean, sleek aesthetic, while the military-grade construction ensures it can handle everyday bumps, drops, and spills without a hitch.

Display and Input

The Chromebook features a 14-inch display with an assumed Full HD resolution of 1920x1080 and a 16:9 aspect ratio, making it adequate for tasks such as web browsing, document editing, or streaming videos. Input devices include a keyboard, touchpad, and integrated webcam. However, it's worth noting that the device does not feature a touchscreen, a potential drawback for those looking for more versatile input options.

Performance and Battery Life

Powered by an Intel Celeron N4500 processor, the device is equipped with 4GB of RAM and 32GB of eMMC storage. This configuration is sufficient for light productivity tasks and Chrome OS's cloud-based ecosystem. Samsung claims up to 12 hours of battery life on a single charge, making the Chromebook Go a dependable companion for all-day use without needing frequent recharges.

Connectivity and Features

The Samsung Galaxy Chromebook Go supports multiple connectivity options, including Bluetooth, HDMI, USB, and Wi-Fi 6. Wi-Fi 6 offers three times faster speeds than its predecessor, ensuring a snappy internet experience for streaming, downloads, and cloud-based workflows. Additionally, the Chromebook's integration with the Phone Hub allows seamless file sharing and control of your Android phone directly from the device.

Final Remarks

In conclusion, the Samsung Galaxy Chromebook Go is a well-rounded device tailored for portability and durability. Although it may not suit power users due to its limited internal storage and lack of a touchscreen, it shines in scenarios that require efficient everyday computing and extended battery life. The inclusion of Wi-Fi 6 and a rugged design make it particularly valuable for students, travelers, or those needing a device that thrives in a mobile environment.

Consider this Chromebook if you are looking for an affordable, tough, and lightweight machine that integrates well with the Chrome OS and Android ecosystem.
