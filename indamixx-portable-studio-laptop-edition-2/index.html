Indamixx Portable Studio Laptop Edition 2

Brand: Indamixx
Operating System: Transmission
Display: 10.2" Backlit LED Matte (1024x600)
Dimensions (WxDxH): 10.23" x 7.08" x 1.25"
Weight: 2.6 lbs
Processor: 1.6 GHz Intel Atom N270
Memory: 2 GB DDR2 (max: 2 GB)
Storage: 160 GB HDD
Battery life: 4 hours
WLAN: WiFi 802.11g
LAN: 10/100 Mbit Ethernet
Colors: Black
Input Devices: Keyboard, Touchpad, Microphone, Video Camera
Connectivity: USB, SD Card reader

The Indamixx Portable Studio Laptop is a netbook targeted at musicians who need a mobile studio for creating, mixing, and editing audio. The 2nd edition of the Portable Studio Laptop is based on the MSI Wind netbook in contrast to the first edition, which is based on the Sylvania G Meso.

The Indamixx Laptop is equipped with an 1.6 GHz Intel Atom processor, 2 GB RAM, 160 GB hard disk drive, Intel 945 Express graphics chipset, a multi card reader, 1 x VGA port, 3 x USB ports, 10/100 Ethernet, WiFi 802.11g, 1x headphone line out port, 1 x microphone line in port, stereo speakers. The netbook measures 10.23" x 7.08" x 1.25" (L x W x H) and weighs 2.6 Pounds. The battery is stated to last up to 4 hours.

The Indamixx Laptop ships with the Ubuntu 9.04 based Transmission 3.0 Linux distribution, which is designed for doing music production. It comes with a newly-updated real-time kernel that combined with the real-time JACK offers excellent low-latency performance.

Some of the bundled applications and tools include:

  • energyXT: a commercial application for recording, editing and mixing music
  • ArdourXchange: a commercial application that enables the import of AAF files so you can work with sessions that were created with Pro Tools, Logic, or Nuendo
  • More than 350 classic samples and scratches
  • Over 260 Plugins and effects
  • Complimentary Software Support
