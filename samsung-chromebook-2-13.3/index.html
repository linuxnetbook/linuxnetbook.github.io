Samsung Chromebook 2 503C32 K01

Brand: Samsung
Operating System: Chrome OS
Display: 13.3" HD LED Display (1920x1080)
Dimensions (WxDxH): 12.72" x 8.8" x 0.65"
Weight: 3.09 lbs
Processor: 2.0 GHz Samsung Exynos 5 Octa 5800
Memory: 4 GB DDR3L
Storage: 16 GB SSD
Battery life: 8.5 hours
WLAN: Wi-Fi 802.11 a/c
Colors: Luminous Titan Gray
Input Devices: Keyboard, Touchpad, Microphone, Video Camera
Connectivity: Bluetooth, USB, SD Card reader

In early March the next generation of the Samsung Chromebook was announced, now it is available for pre-order on and is expected to ship in June.

The 13.3-inch model of the Samsung Chromebook 2 features several hardware improvements compared to its predecessor, most notably a larger full HD display with a maximum resolution of 1920 x 1080 pixels. Moreover, the new model is powered by a 2.0 GHz Samsung Exynos 5 Octa 5800 octo-core processor, comes with 4 GB RAM, 16 GB SSD, 720p HD webcam, and features an elegant leather-like stitched design.

The slim and lightweight Chrome OS laptop offers fast Wi-Fi 802.11 a/c speeds and up to 8.5 hours of battery life and is well suited for many mobile computing tasks. The Chrome operating system boots in 10 seconds and the laptop wakes up in 1 second.

The improved hardware comes at a price. With an initial price of US $399.99 the 13.3" Samsung Chromebook 2 is one of the more "costly" Chromebooks. Also see the 11.6" Samsung Chromebook 2.
