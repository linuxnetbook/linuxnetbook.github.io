ASUS Chromebook C423 C423NA-IS44F

Brand: ASUS
Operating System: Chrome OS
Display: 14.0" Full HD Anti-Glare NanoEdge (1920x1080)
Dimensions (WxDxH): 12.7" x 9.0" x 0.6"
Weight: 2.95 lbs
Processor: 2.4 GHz Intel Celeron N3350
Memory: 4 GB LPDDR4
Storage: 64 GB eMMC
WLAN: Dual-band 802.11ac Wi-Fi
Colors: Silver
Input Devices: Keyboard, Touchpad, Webcam
Connectivity: Bluetooth 4.2, HDMI, USB 3.2 Type-C, USB 3.2 Type-A, MicroSD Card Reader, Wi-Fi (Dual-band 802.11ac)

The ASUS Chromebook C423 is a portable and well-rounded option for users who want practicality and affordability. Designed for on-the-go productivity, this 14-inch Chromebook features a sharp Full HD NanoEdge Anti-Glare display with an ultra-thin bezel, making it perfect for reducing screen glare and enhancing viewing clarity. The durable 180-degree hinge enables flexible use, allowing the screen to lay flat for easier collaboration or sharing content with others.

Powered by an Intel Dual-Core Celeron N3350 processor (2M Cache, up to 2.4 GHz), the Chromebook offers reliable performance for basic computing tasks. It is equipped with 4GB of LPDDR4 RAM and 64GB of eMMC storage, providing adequate memory and space for everyday use. While the storage capacity may seem limited, the inclusion of a MicroSD card slot allows for easy expansion.

Connectivity is comprehensive, featuring 2x USB 3.2 Type-C, 2x USB 3.2 Type-A, a Micro SD card reader, and a 3.5mm audio jack. For wireless communication, the device supports Bluetooth 4.2 and dual-band Wi-Fi 5 (802.11ac) for seamless internet connectivity.

Weighing just 2.95 lbs and measuring only 0.6 inches thick, the C423 is highly portable. It is a perfect companion for students, remote workers, or anyone looking for a lightweight laptop that delivers reliable performance without breaking the bank.

However, it is worth noting that this Chromebook does not feature a touchscreen and is primarily designed for light workloads, web browsing, and app use through Chrome OS. If you're in need of a straightforward yet functional laptop for your daily tasks, the ASUS Chromebook C423 is worth considering.
