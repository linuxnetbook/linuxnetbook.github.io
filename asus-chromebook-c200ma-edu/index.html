ASUS Chromebook C200MA-EDU

Brand: ASUS
Operating System: Chrome OS
Display: 11.6" HD LED display (1366x768)
Dimensions (WxDxH): 12" x 7.9" x 0.8"
Weight: 2.5 lbs
Processor: 2.16 GHz Intel Celeron N2830
Memory: 4 GB DDR3L SDRAM
Storage: 32 GB eMMC
Battery life: 11 hours
WLAN: Wi-Fi 802.11a/b/g/n/ac
Colors: Black
Input Devices: Keyboard, Touchpad, Microphone, Video Camera
Connectivity: USB, SD Card reader

The ASUS Chromebook C200MA-EDU is a 11.6-inch laptop with 4GB RAM and 32GB eMMC storage powered by a 2.16 GHz dual-core Intel Bay Trail N2830 processor.

The C200MA-EDU is equipped with a 1.2MP camera, 1x USB 3.0, 1x USB 2.0, HDMI, SD card slot(SDXC), Bluetooth 4.0, 802.11 A/C wireless and offers up to 11 hours of battery life.

The ASUS laptop running Google's Chrome OS was offered for an initial price of $259.99. An interesting product, if you need great mobility and a little more power than many of the alternative Chromebooks to choose from.
