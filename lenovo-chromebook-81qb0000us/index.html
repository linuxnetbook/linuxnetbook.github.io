The Lenovo 100E Chromebook (2nd Gen) is tailored towards users in need of a lightweight, budget-friendly device for basic tasks and educational purposes. Powered by the MediaTek MT8173C processor and featuring 4GB of DDR4 RAM, it aims to deliver consistent performance for everyday demands.

Key Specifications:

  • Processor: MediaTek MT8173C, Quad-Core Cortex-A53 (up to 2.0GHz)
  • Graphics: Powervr GX6250 (UHD Graphics)
  • Display: 11.6-inch HD (1366x768), TN panel (not touchscreen)
  • Memory: 4GB DDR4 RAM
  • Storage: 32GB eMMC
  • Connectivity: Wi-Fi 802.11ac, Bluetooth, HDMI, USB
  • Battery Life: Up to 10 hours
  • Weight: 2.68 lbs
  • Color: Black

Build and Design

The 100E is compact and robust, making it an ideal choice for students. Its slim profile (0.78 inches in height) and lightweight design (2.68 lbs) make it convenient to carry around. The rugged chassis is built to withstand the wear and tear of daily usage, particularly in classrooms or when traveling.

Performance and Usability

Equipped with a quad-core processor and 4GB of RAM, this Chromebook efficiently handles basic tasks such as browsing the web, document editing, and media playback. Its 11.6-inch TN display, while not the sharpest on the market, meets the needs of casual users with reasonable clarity, and the device’s eMMC storage ensures quick boot times.

Connectivity and Input Options

The Lenovo 100E features versatile connectivity, including Wi-Fi 802.11ac, Bluetooth, HDMI, and USB ports. Input devices include a sturdy keyboard, a responsive touchpad, and a built-in webcam suitable for video conferencing or online classes.

Ideal for Rugged and Educational Use

Released in 2019, the Lenovo 100E Chromebook (2nd Gen) is designed with educational institutions in mind. The combination of hardware and Chrome OS ensures software updates and security features for an extended period, making it a reliable option for learning environments.

Limitations

While the Lenovo 100E is affordable and durable, its limitations include modest storage (32GB eMMC), a non-touch TN display, and performance that may struggle with intensive applications or multitasking.

If you’re searching for a cost-effective, resilient device for basic tasks, the Lenovo 100E Chromebook (2nd Gen) is worth considering. However, power users and those with a need for greater storage or resolution might need to explore alternatives.
