Acer Chromebook Plus 515 CB515-2HT-33M4 Laptop with Google AI

Brand: Acer
Operating System: Chrome OS
Display: 15.6" IPS Touch (1920x1080)
Dimensions (WxDxH): 14.2" x 9.33" x 0.79"
Touch Screen: Yes
Weight: 3.7 lbs
Processor: 4.5 GHz Intel Core i3-1305U
Memory: 8 GB LPDDR5X (max: 8 GB)
Storage: 256 GB PCIe Gen4 SSD
Battery life: 10 hours
WLAN: Intel Wireless Wi-Fi 6E AX211 with dual-stream Wi-Fi (2.4GHz, 5GHz, 6GHz) and 2x2 MU-MIMO support
Colors: Gray
Input Devices: Keyboard, Touchpad, Webcam, Touchscreen
Connectivity: Bluetooth 5.1, HDMI 1.4, USB Type-C, USB, Wi-Fi 6E

The Acer Chromebook Plus 515 (CB515-2HT-33M4) is a feature-packed 15.6-inch laptop designed for professionals, students, and light gamers alike. It combines the strength of Intel's 13th Gen Core i3-1305U processor with the versatility of ChromeOS, making it an excellent choice for those who value speed, security, and simplicity.

Key Features and Specifications

Powerful Performance

Under the hood, the Chromebook Plus 515 is equipped with the Intel Core i3-1305U processor, clocking up to 4.5 GHz with 6 cores. Combined with 8GB of LPDDR5X RAM and 256GB of PCIe Gen4 SSD storage, this laptop delivers seamless multitasking for work and play.

Immersive Display

The 15.6-inch Full HD (1920 x 1080) IPS touchscreen provides vibrant visuals with excellent viewing angles. The widescreen "ComfyView" design ensures reduced glare, making it suitable for extended use.

Enhanced Connectivity

Modern connectivity options include Wi-Fi 6E (Intel Wireless AX211) with multi-band support and Bluetooth 5.1 for fast and reliable wireless connections. Physical ports include:

  • Two USB Type-C (USB 3.2 Gen 1) with DisplayPort support and USB charging
  • One USB-A (USB 3.2 Gen 1)
  • One HDMI 1.4 port

Productivity and Security

ChromeOS offers a lightweight yet powerful platform, preloaded with Google apps like Docs, Sheets, and Gmail, alongside compatibility with Microsoft 365 and Adobe Photoshop (subscription required). Built-in Google AI tools, like the "Gemini" assistant, enhance productivity with features for brainstorming and content generation. The Titan C2 security chip ensures industry-leading protection.

Portability and Battery Life

Weighing 3.7 pounds and measuring just 0.79 inches thick, the Chromebook Plus 515 remains portable despite its large display. With up to 10 hours of battery life, it easily lasts through a full day of intensive tasks.

AI-Powered Upsides

Purchasing this Chromebook includes a 12-month subscription to the Google One AI Premium Plan, offering 2TB of cloud storage, Gemini Advanced usage, and enhanced productivity across Gmail and Google Docs.

Summary

The Acer Chromebook Plus 515 strikes a balance between productivity, entertainment, and modern AI tools. Whether you need a device for drafting documents, remote learning, or casual gaming, this Chromebook consistently delivers a reliable and secure experience.
