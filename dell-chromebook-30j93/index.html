Dell Chromebook 3100 30J93 Touchscreen Laptop

Brand: Dell
Operating System: Chrome OS
Display: 11.6" HD Touchscreen (1366x768)
Dimensions (WxDxH): 8.2" x 12" x 0.8"
Touch Screen: Yes
Weight: 3.39 lbs
Processor: 2.6 GHz Intel Celeron N4000
Memory: 4 GB LPDDR4
Storage: 32 GB eMMC
Battery life: 9 hours
WLAN: Intel Dual Band Wireless-AC 9560 (2x2)
Colors: Black
Input Devices: Keyboard, Touchpad, Webcam, Microphone
Connectivity: Bluetooth 5.0, HDMI, USB, USB Type-C, Wi-Fi 802.11AC

The Dell Chromebook 3100 (model 30J93) is a compact and durable laptop, tailored for daily computing tasks. Running Chrome OS, it offers a fast and secure environment, ideal for students, light productivity tasks, or anyone looking for a budget-friendly device. Here's a closer look at its key specifications and features:

Performance

At the heart of the Dell Chromebook 3100 is the Intel Celeron N4000 Processor. With 2 cores, a clock speed of up to 2.6GHz, 4MB cache, and a low power consumption (6W), this processor ensures smooth performance for basic tasks like web browsing, streaming, and document editing. It is supported by 4GB of LPDDR4 RAM (2400MHz), which provides decent multitasking capabilities.

Storage

The device comes with 32GB of eMMC storage. While this capacity is modest, it is sufficient for lightweight tasks and applications, especially given Chrome OS's cloud-first focus.

Display

The Chromebook features an 11.6-inch HD touchscreen display with a resolution of 1366 x 768 pixels, providing clear visuals for work or entertainment. The embedded touch capability allows for intuitive interactions with Chrome OS.

Connectivity and I/O

The Dell Chromebook 3100 offers a variety of connectivity options:

  • 2 USB Type-C ports
  • 2 USB 3.1 ports
  • Wi-Fi AC 9560 (802.11AC, 2x2) for fast internet connections
  • Bluetooth 5.0 for peripherals and wireless accessories

Build and Input Devices

Weighing 3.39 lbs and with dimensions of 0.8 x 8.2 x 12.0 inches, this device is compact and designed to withstand daily wear and tear. It is equipped with:

  • A keyboard
  • A touchpad
  • A webcam for video conferencing
  • An integrated microphone

Who Is It For?

Released in April 2019, the Dell Chromebook 3100 is a reliable machine for those needing a budget-friendly, portable, and durable laptop. It's perfect for students or users looking for a device for everyday tasks without hefty demands on hardware.

This Chromebook's combination of a responsive touchscreen, decent performance, and lightweight build makes it a versatile choice for anyone in need of a dependable companion for simple day-to-day computing needs.
