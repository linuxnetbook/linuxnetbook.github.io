Lenovo N21 Chromebook 80MG0001US

Brand: Lenovo
Operating System: Chrome OS
Display: 11.6" Anti-Glare Screen (1366x768)

WLAN: Wi-Fi 802.11 a/c
Colors: Black
Input Devices: Keyboard, Touchpad, Microphone, Video Camera
Connectivity: Bluetooth, USB, SD Card reader

The Lenovo N21 Chromebook 80MG0001US is an 11.6-inch laptop powered by a 2.58 GHz dual-core Intel Celeron N2840 processor. It comes with 4 GB RAM, 16 GB SSD, 1x USB 2.0 and 1x USB 3.0 ports, HDMI port, a 720p HD webcam, integrated speakers, Bluetooth and Wi-Fi 802.11 a/c.

The N21 is designed for education. It has a rotatable camera and an integrated microphone, that rotates with the camera to better capture sound. The anti-glare screen produces less reflection under fluorescent lights and near windows, reducing eyestrain. The serviceable battery lasts up to 9.5 hours and can be replaced by school IT. The N21 also comes with a retractable handle, that makes it easy to carry and less likely to be dropped.

The recommended retail price price of the Lenovo N21 Chromebook was $239 when it was released in early 2015. There are several offers on Amazon.com well below the RRP.
