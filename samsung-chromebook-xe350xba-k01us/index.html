Samsung Chromebook XE350XBA-K01US 15.6-inch Full HD Laptop

Brand: Samsung
Operating System: Chrome OS
Display: 15.6" Full HD (1920x1080)
Dimensions (WxDxH): 14.16" x 9.64" x 0.65"
Weight: 3.8 lbs
Processor: 1.1 GHz Intel Celeron N4000
Memory: 4 GB
Storage: 32 GB eMMC
Battery life: 10.5 hours
WLAN: Gigabit Wi-Fi
Colors: Silver
Input Devices: Keyboard, Touchpad, Webcam
Connectivity: Bluetooth, HDMI, USB, Wi-Fi

The Samsung XE350XBA-K01US Chromebook 4 is a compact yet highly functional device that caters to users looking for simplicity, durability, and speed. It was released on October 7, 2019, and runs on Chrome OS.

Key Features

  1. 15.6-inch Full HD Display: The large, immersive display supports a maximum resolution of 1920x1080, providing clear and sharp visuals. Despite the screen size, the Chromebook maintains a compact frame.
  2. Durability: Built to last, the device boasts military-grade durability, making it an ideal choice for both everyday users and those frequently on the go.
  3. Connectivity: Equipped with Gigabit Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, USB ports, and HDMI, the Chromebook ensures you stay connected and productive, whether working or streaming.
  4. Battery Life: A long-lasting battery provides up to 10.5 hours of usage, ensuring you can get through a full day without needing a recharge.
  5. Advanced Security: With multiple layers of built-in virus protection, your data remains safe and secure. Chrome OS also comes with automatic updates to keep the system optimized.
  6. Performance: Powered by the Intel Celeron Processor N4000 and 4GB of RAM, this machine is sufficient for basic multitasking and running lightweight applications. Its 32GB eMMC storage offers quick access to your files and apps.
  7. Google Assistant Integration: The Chromebook comes enabled with Google Assistant, allowing voice commands for ease of use.

Build & Design

At just 0.65 inches thick and weighing 3.8 lbs, the Samsung Chromebook stands out for its portability. The sleek silver finish adds a professional and polished look. Input options include a keyboard, touchpad, and webcam.

While the device does not support touchscreen functionality, the inclusion of a Full HD display ensures a vibrant visual experience.

Verdict

The Samsung XE350XBA-K01US Chromebook 4 provides excellent value as a lightweight, durable, and reliable Chrome OS device. It's best suited for students, casual users, or professionals who need a simple yet effective tool for everyday tasks. However, with limited storage space and no touchscreen, it may be less ideal for those requiring heavy multitasking or advanced functionality.
