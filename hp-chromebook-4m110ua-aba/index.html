HP Chromebook 4M110UA#ABA - Thin & Portable 14-inch Laptop

Brand: HP
Operating System: Chrome OS
Display: 14" Micro-edge HD (1366x768)
Dimensions (WxDxH): 12.82" x 8.6" x 0.7"
Weight: 3.24 lbs
Processor: 1.1 GHz Intel Celeron N4020
Memory: 4 GB DDR4
Storage: 32 GB eMMC
Battery life: 13.5 hours
WLAN: Wi-Fi 5
Colors: Ceramic white
Input Devices: Keyboard, Touchpad, Multi-touch touchpad, Webcam
Connectivity: Bluetooth 5.0, HDMI, USB, Wi-Fi 5

The HP Chromebook 14 (model 4M110UA#ABA) is a lightweight Chromebook designed to meet the demands of home, school, and work tasks. With its compact design and robust hardware, it delivers solid performance for everyday productivity and entertainment.

Key Specifications

  • Processor: Powered by a dual-core Intel Celeron N4020 processor, this Chromebook balances performance, efficiency, and affordability.
  • Display: The 14-inch HD (1366x768) micro-edge display delivers clear visuals, making it ideal for streaming, browsing, or document editing.
  • Graphics: Intel UHD Graphics 600 enables the device to smoothly stream 4K content and handle basic next-gen games.
  • Memory and Storage: Equipped with 4 GB of DDR4 RAM and 32 GB eMMC storage, it offers sufficient capacity for documents, apps, and lightweight multitasking.
  • Battery Life: You can work or play throughout the day with up to 13.5 hours of battery life. The HP Fast Charge feature recharges the battery to 50% in just 45 minutes.
  • Operating System: Runs on Chrome OS, providing a secure and efficient environment with access to Android apps via the Google Play Store.

Design and Connectivity

  • Portability: Lightweight at just 3.24 lbs and measuring 0.7 inches in height, the HP Chromebook 14 is ideal for on-the-go users.
  • Connectivity Options: Offers robust connectivity with Wi-Fi 5, Bluetooth 5.0, USB ports, and an HDMI port for external displays.
  • Input Devices: Features a full-size keyboard and a multi-touch touchpad supporting four-finger gestures for easy navigation. A built-in webcam is also available for virtual meetings or online classes.

Sustainability and Warranty

HP emphasizes environmental responsibility with this ENERGY STAR-certified and EPEAT Silver-registered Chromebook. Additionally, the device is backed by a 1-year limited hardware warranty, giving users peace of mind.

Who Is It For?

The HP Chromebook 14 is a practical choice for students, professionals, or anyone seeking a reliable laptop for web-based tasks, media streaming, and light productivity. Its portability and long battery life make it especially suitable for users who are frequently on the move.

This Chromebook exemplifies HP's commitment to blending efficiency, portability, and sustainability in a sleek package. While it's not built for heavy computational tasks or gaming, it excels in its intended use case as a versatile, everyday device.
