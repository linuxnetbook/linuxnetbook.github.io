ASUS Chromebook C300MA-DH01

Brand: ASUS
Operating System: Chrome OS
Display: 13.3" HD LED display (1366x768)
Dimensions (WxDxH): 13" x 9.1" x 0.8"
Weight: 3.1 lbs
Processor: 2.16 GHz Intel Celeron N2830
Memory: 2 GB DDR3L SDRAM
Storage: 16 GB eMMC
Battery life: 10 hours
WLAN: Wi-Fi 802.11a/b/g/n/ac
Colors: Light Blue, Red, Yellow
Input Devices: Keyboard, Touchpad, Microphone, Video Camera
Connectivity: USB, SD Card reader

The ASUS Chromebook C300MA-DH01 is an 13.3-inch laptop powered a 2.16 GHz dual-core Intel Bay Trail N2830 processor, that comes with 2GB of RAM and 16 GB eMMC storage.

The C300MA-DH01 is equipped with an HD camera, 1x USB 3.0, 1x USB 2.0, HDMI port, SD card slot(SDXC), Bluetooth 4.0, 802.11 A/C wireless and offers up to 10 hours of battery life.
