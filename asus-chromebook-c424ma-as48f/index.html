ASUS Chromebook C424 C424MA-AS48F

Brand: ASUS
Operating System: Chrome OS
Display: 14" FHD Anti-Glare NanoEdge (1920x1080)
Dimensions (WxDxH): 12.7" x 8.98" x 0.71"
Weight: 2.87 lbs
Processor: 2.8 GHz Intel Dual-Core Celeron N4020
Memory: 4 GB LPDDR4
Storage: 128 GB eMMC
Battery life: 10 hours
Colors: Silver
Input Devices: Keyboard
Connectivity: USB Type-C (Gen 1), USB Type-A

The ASUS C424MA-AS48F Chromebook offers an efficient and lightweight solution for those looking to experience a Linux-based operating system without delving too deep into technical setups. This Chromebook runs on Chrome OS, an operating system developed by Google that's tailored for today's fast-paced digital lifestyle.

Key Features

  • Display: The laptop boasts a 14-inch Full HD (1920x1080) Anti-Glare NanoEdge display. Its design includes an ultra-narrow bezel and a 180-degree hinge, which makes content sharing and viewing comfortable from various angles.
  • Performance: It's powered by the Intel Dual-Core Celeron N4020 Processor with a 4M cache and speeds up to 2.8 GHz. This setup ensures a smooth and responsive performance, suitable for everyday tasks.
  • Memory and Storage: The device is equipped with 4GB of LPDDR4 RAM and 128GB of eMMC storage. This setup provides sufficient space for essential applications and documents while maintaining fast operation speeds.
  • Connectivity: It includes USB Type-C and Type-A ports, ensuring compatibility with various peripherals and devices.
  • Operating System: The Chromebook runs on Chrome OS, which is known for its security features such as built-in virus protection and automated updates. The OS boots up in seconds and offers a seamless experience over time.
  • Google Integration: Standard Google applications are included, allowing users to edit and manage documents with Google Docs, Sheets, and Slides. The laptop also supports a wide array of applications through the Google Play Store.

ASUS's C424MA Chromebook is an option worth considering for those interested in a versatile laptop running a reliable and secure operating system. With its feature set and integration with Google services, it's poised to cater to both personal and professional needs efficiently.
