ASUS Chromebook C300SA-DS02

Brand: ASUS
Operating System: Chrome OS
Display: 13.3" LED backlit HD glossy screen (1366x768)
Dimensions (WxDxH): 13" x 9.1" x 0.9"
Weight: 2.98 lbs
Processor: 1.6 GHz Intel Celeron N3060
Memory: 4 GB LPDDR3
Storage: 16 GB eMMC
Battery life: 10 hours
WLAN: Wi-Fi 802.11 a/c
Colors: Black, Light Blue, Red
Input Devices: Keyboard, Touchpad, Microphone, Video Camera
Connectivity: Bluetooth, USB, SD Card reader

The ASUS Chromebook C300SA-DS02 is an 13.6-inch laptop powered by the energy-efficient 1.6 GHz dual-core Intel N3060 processor. It is equipped with 4 GB RAM, 16 GB eMMC storage, a 720p HD camera, Wi-Fi 802.11 a/c, Bluetooth 4.2, 2 x USB 3.0 ports, 1 x HDMI port and 1 x SD card slot.

With a battery life of up to 10 hours, weighing less than 3lbs, and sporting a matte finish the ASUS Chromebook C300SA-DS02 is well-suited for mobile and outside usage in both dim and brightly lit environments.

The ASUS Chromebook C300SA-DS02 was offered for an initial price of $229 when released in April 2016.
