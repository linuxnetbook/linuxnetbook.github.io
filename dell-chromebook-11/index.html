Dell Chromebook 11 730-8301

Brand: Dell
Operating System: Chrome OS
Display: 11.6" Backlit LED (1366x768)
Dimensions (WxDxH): 11.6" x 7.9" x 0.97"
Weight: 2.9 lbs
Processor: 1.4 GHz Intel Celeron 2955U
Memory: 4 GB DDR3
Storage: 16 GB SSD
Battery life: 10 hours
WLAN: Wi-Fi 802.11 b/g/n
Colors: Foggy Night
Input Devices: Keyboard, Touchpad, Microphone, Video Camera
Connectivity: Bluetooth, USB, SD Card reader

The Dell Chromebook 11 730-8301 has a 11.6 inch screen with a 1366 by 768 pixel resolution and is powered by a 1.4 GHz dual-core Intel 2955U processor. It is equipped with 16 GB SSD and 4 GB and offers up to 10 hours battery life. The laptop features the typical connectivity options you'd expect from a Chromebook, including Bluetooth 4.0, 2 USB 3.0 ports, HDMI port, full-size SD card slot.

The Dell Chromebook 11 730-8301 was first released in early 2014 targeted at the education market. The laptop cannot be ordered any more from the Dell online shop (newer Chromebook models are available), but there are the following offers on Amazon.com at the time of writing:
