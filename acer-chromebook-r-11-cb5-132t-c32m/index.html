Acer Chromebook R 11 CB5-132T-C32M

Brand: Acer
Operating System: Chrome OS
Display: 11.6" Full-HD IPS LED-backlit Display (1366x768)
Dimensions (WxDxH): 11.57" x 8.03" x 0.76"
Touch Screen: Yes
Weight: 2.76 lbs
Processor: 1.6 GHz Intel Core N3150
Memory: 2 GB DDR3L SDRAM
Storage: 32 GB SSD
Battery life: 10 hours
WLAN: Wi-Fi 802.11 a/c
Colors: White
Input Devices: Keyboard, Touchpad, Microphone, Video Camera
Connectivity: USB, SD Card reader

The Acer Chromebook R 11 CB5-132T-C32M is an 11.6-inch laptop powered by a 1.6 GHz quad-core Intel Celeron N3150 Braswell processor. It comes with 2 GB RAM, 32 GB internal storage, 1x USB 2.0 and 1x USB 3.0 ports, HDMI port, Wi-Fi 802.11 a/c, Bluetooth 4.0, an HD webcam and offers up to 10 hours of battery life.

One highlight of this small and lightweight device is the high-brightness, LED-backlit IPS (In-Plane Switching) with integrated 10-point multitouch screen, which supports finger touch and image auto rotation.

There are not too many small-screen chromebooks with an IPS display not to mention a touchscreen. Other options include the ASUS Chromebook Flip or the HP Chromebook 11-2110nr.

The Acer Chromebook R 11 CB5-132T-C32M had an initial price of $279.99 and it wouldn't surprise me if becomes one of the top-sellers in 2015's holiday season.
