Lenovo N22 Chromebook 80SF0000US

Brand: Lenovo
Operating System: Chrome OS
Display: 11.6" HD anti-glare display (1366x768)
Dimensions (WxDxH): 11.83" x 8.35" x 0.86"
Weight: 2.7 lbs
Processor: 1.6 GHz Intel Braswell N3050
Memory: 2 GB DDR3
Storage: 16 GB eMMC
Battery life: 14 hours
WLAN: Wi-Fi 802.11 a/c
Colors: Black
Input Devices: Keyboard, Touchpad, Microphone, Video Camera
Connectivity: Bluetooth, USB, 2-in-1 Card Reader

The Lenovo N22 Chromebook 80SF is an 11.6-inch laptop powered by a 1.6 GHz dual-core Intel Braswell N3050 processor. It comes with 2 GB RAM, 16 GB storage, 2x USB 3.0 ports, 1x HDMI port, a 720p HD rotatable webcam, integrated speakers, Bluetooth 4.1 and Wi-Fi 802.11 a/c.

Similar to other Lenovo laptops listed on this site, the N22 Chromebook is targeted at students. It is on the lower end of the power and price spectrums, has a semi-rugged design and a water-resistant keyboard. A special feature is the built-in webcam, that can rotate 180°, so you can film yourself while chatting or your surroundings.

The Lenovo N22 Chromebook 80SF was released in March 2016 for a start price around US $180. For current offers in the US check out this product page on Amazon.com.
