Acer Chromebook 11 C740 C4PE

Brand: Acer
Operating System: Chrome OS
Display: 11.6" Active Matrix TFT Color LCD (1366x768)
Dimensions (WxDxH): 11.3" x 8" x 0.8"
Weight: 2.87 lbs
Processor: 1.5 GHz Intel Celeron 3205U
Memory: 4 GB DDR3L SDRAM
Storage: 16 GB SSD
Battery life: 9 hours
WLAN: Wi-Fi 802.11a/b/g/n/ac
Colors: Black
Input Devices: Keyboard, Touchpad, Microphone, Video Camera
Connectivity: USB, SD Card reader

The Acer Chromebook 11 C740 C4PE is an 11.6-inch screen laptop that comes with a 1.5 GHz dual-core Intel Celeron 3205U processor, 4 GB RAM, 16 GB SSD, 802.11AC Wi-Fi, a built-in webcam, 1 x USB 3.0 and 1 x USB 2.0 ports, HDMI port and a 3-Cell Li-Polymer battery (3920 mAh) that offers up to 9 hours of battery life. The recommended retail price (RPP) is $279.99.
