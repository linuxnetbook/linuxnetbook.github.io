The Acer Chromebook 15 CB5-571 sports a 15.6-inch screen and is powered by a 1.5 GHz dual-core Intel Celeron 3205U processor. It comes with 4 GB RAM, 16 GB SSD, 1x USB 2.0 and 1x USB 3.0 ports, HDMI port, Wi-Fi 802.11 a/c, Bluetooth 4.0, a 1.3MP webcam and offers up to 9 hours of battery life.

The Acer Chromebook 15 CB5-571 C1DZ was released in March 2015 for an initial price of $299.99. For current offers in the US, see this Amazon.com product page.
