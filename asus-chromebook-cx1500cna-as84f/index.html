ASUS Chromebook CX1500CNA-AS84F CX1 - 15.6-inch Full HD Laptop

Brand: ASUS
Operating System: Chrome OS
Display: 15.6" NanoEdge (1920x1080)
Dimensions (WxDxH): 14.22" x 9.84" x 0.74"
Weight: 3.97 lbs
Processor: 2.4 GHz Intel Celeron N3350
Memory: 8 GB DDR4
Storage: 64 GB eMMC
Battery life: 11 hours
WLAN: Wi-Fi 5
Colors: Transparent Silver
Input Devices: Keyboard (QWERTY layout), Touchpad, Webcam
Connectivity: Bluetooth 4.2, HDMI, USB Type-C (Gen 1), USB 3.2 Type-A (Gen 1), Wi-Fi 5, Micro SD Card reader

The ASUS CX1500CNA-AS84F Chromebook CX1 is a budget-friendly solution for users looking for a lightweight, durable laptop running Chrome OS. Let's break down its features:

Performance and Core Specs

At the heart of the ASUS CX1 is the Intel Celeron N3350 processor, a dual-core CPU with 2MB cache and clock speeds up to 2.4 GHz. Complemented by 8GB of DDR4 RAM and 64GB of eMMC storage, this Chromebook is built for basic multitasking and web-based productivity.

Display

The device sports a 15.6-inch Full HD (1920x1080) NanoEdge display, delivering sharp visuals in a slightly larger-than-average form factor. However, it lacks touchscreen capability.

Build Quality and Portability

Weighing 3.97 lbs and built to U.S. military-grade durability standards (MIL-STD 810H), the ASUS CX1 offers robust portability. It measures 0.74 inches thick, 14.22 inches wide, and 9.84 inches deep, making it suitable for on-the-go users.

Connectivity and Input Options

The Chromebook includes an array of ports and connectivity options:

  • 2 USB Type-C (Gen 1) ports
  • 2 USB 3.2 Type-A (Gen 1) ports
  • 1 audio jack
  • Micro SD card reader
  • Bluetooth 4.2
  • Wi-Fi 5

Its input devices include a standard QWERTY keyboard, a precise touchpad, and a built-in webcam.

Operating System and Features

Running Chrome OS, the CX1 supports Google apps for editing documents and files in Google Docs, Sheets, and Slides. It also offers built-in storage for offline file access and includes a 12-month trial of Google One with extra Gmail, Google Drive, and Google Photos storage. Note that this Google offer expired on 1/31/2023, so check availability for new users.

Battery Life

The advertised battery life is up to 11 hours under typical usage conditions, though your mileage may vary depending on workload and settings.

Final Thoughts

The ASUS CX1500CNA-AS84F Chromebook CX1 is a competent option for users needing a reliable and durable laptop for web browsing, online work, and lightweight multitasking. Its combination of a 15.6-inch display, military-grade durability, and Chrome OS integration makes it suited for education or general use, though the lack of a touchscreen and moderate processing power could limit more advanced use cases.
