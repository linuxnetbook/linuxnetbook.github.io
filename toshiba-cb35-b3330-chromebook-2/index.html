Toshiba CB35-B3330 Chromebook 2

Brand: Toshiba
Operating System: Chrome OS
Display: 13.3" HD Display (1366x768)
Dimensions (WxDxH): 12.6" x 8.4" x 0.76"
Weight: 3 lbs
Processor: 2.16 GHz Intel Celeron N2840
Memory: 2 GB DDR3L SDRAM
Storage: 16 GB SSD
Battery life: 11 hours
WLAN: Wi-Fi 802.11 a/c
Colors: Silver
Input Devices: Keyboard, Touchpad, Microphone, Video Camera
Connectivity: USB, SD Card reader

The CB35-B3330 Chromebook is Toshiba's lower-spec successor of the CB35-B3120 released in early 2014. The most obvious improvement is a more powerful 2.16 GHz Intel Celeron N2840 dual-core processor.

The CB35-B3330 has a 13.3 inch screen with 1366 x 768 pixel resolution, comes with 2 GB RAM, a 16 GB solid-state drive, 1 x USB 2.0 and 1 x USB 3.0 ports, HDMI port, SD card slot and built-in dual-array microphones and an HD webcam. The Toshiba CB35-B3330 weighs only 3 pounds and is rated at 11 hours of battery life.

If you want a more powerful laptop with a full HD screen, Toshiba got you covered with their higher-spec CB35-B3340 Chromebook 2. Check out the side-by-side comparison of the CB35-B3330 and CB35-B3340 Chromebooks.
