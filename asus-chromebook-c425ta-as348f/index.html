ASUS Chromebook C425 C425TA-AS348F Clamshell Laptop

Brand: ASUS
Operating System: Chrome OS
Display: 14" IPS (1920x1080)
Dimensions (WxDxH): 12.67" x 8.19" x 0.67"
Weight: 2.8 lbs
Processor: 3.4 GHz Intel Core m3-8100Y Processor
Memory: 4 GB LPDDR3
Storage: 128 GB eMMC
WLAN: 802.11ac
Input Devices: Backlit Keyboard, Trackpad
Connectivity:

The ASUS Chromebook C425 C425TA-AS348F is a sleek and lightweight option for users seeking a reliable Linux-based operating system. With a 14-inch Full HD IPS display, the laptop offers a spectacular viewing experience by fitting a large screen into a compact 13-inch footprint. This design, combined with a substantial six-inch trackpad, makes it both portable and easy to use.

Under the hood, the device is powered by an Intel Core m3-8100Y Processor, ensuring snappy performance for multitasking and everyday tasks. It is equipped with 4GB of LPDDR3 RAM and 128GB of eMMC storage, providing decent capacity for running applications and storing files. Connectivity options include two USB Type-C and one Type-A ports, and a backlit keyboard enhances usability in dim lighting.

Particularly noteworthy is the laptop's 180-degree hinge, allowing flexible usage scenarios. At just 2.8 pounds, its lightweight build and stylish design mean it's easy to carry without sacrificing aesthetics.

Running on Chrome OS, the ASUS Chromebook C425 integrates well with Google's ecosystem. Users can access and edit documents seamlessly using built-in Google apps.
