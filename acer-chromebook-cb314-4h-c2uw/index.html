The Acer Chromebook 314 CB314-4H-C2UW offers users a sleek design combined with the efficiency of Chrome OS, making it suitable for both productivity tasks and entertainment. Running on an Intel Processor N100, this laptop is built to handle everyday tasks smoothly.

Key Features

  • Operating System: The Chromebook runs on Chrome OS, providing quick access to an array of Google services including Gmail, Docs, and more.
  • Performance: Equipped with the Intel Processor N100 (up to 3.4GHz) and Intel UHD Graphics, it ensures a smooth computing experience.
  • Display: The 14.0" Full HD Widescreen ComfyView LED-backlit IPS Display delivers clear and vibrant visuals at a resolution of 1920 x 1080.
  • Memory & Storage: Offers 4GB LPDDR5 RAM and 64GB eMMC storage, suitable for everyday use and cloud-based tasks.
  • Connectivity: Includes Intel Wireless Wi-Fi 6E AX211 for seamless internet connection, alongside Bluetooth 5.1 or above. Connectivity options are enhanced with two USB Type-C ports and two USB 3.2 Gen 1 Type A ports.
  • Audio & Camera: Features two built-in upward-firing stereo speakers and an HD Facial Auto Exposure Camera with 720p video capabilities.

Additional Benefits

  • Security: Leveraging built-in protection and the Titan C2 security chip, the Chromebook ensures your data and privacy are safeguarded.
  • Battery Life: Enjoy up to 10.5 hours of battery life, enabling you to work and play throughout the day without constant recharges.
  • App Accessibility: Access popular apps through Google Play, including Microsoft 365 and Adobe Express.

The Acer Chromebook 314 combines practicality with performance, making it an ideal choice for users who value efficiency and ease of use.
