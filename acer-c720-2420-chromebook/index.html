Acer C720-2420 Chromebook

Brand: Acer
Operating System: Chrome OS
Display: 11.6" HD Widescreen LED-backlit Display (1366x768)
Dimensions (WxDxH): 11.34" x 8.03" x 0.75"
Weight: 2.76 lbs
Processor: 1.4 GHz Intel Celeron 2995U
Memory: 2 GB DDR3 SDRAM
Storage: 32 GB SSD
Battery life: 8.5 hours
WLAN: Wi-Fi 802.11 a/b/g/n
Colors: Granite Gray
Input Devices: Keyboard, Touchpad, Microphone, Video Camera
Connectivity: USB, SD Card reader

The Acer C720-2420 Chromebook is the successor of the Acer C710 and comes with several improvements. The most notable one for me is the vastly improved battery life of up to 8.5 hours. With 2.76 lbs it also weighs slightly less, so certainly better mobility with this model.

Apart from the CPU the rest of the hardware specs don't differ too much: Intel Celeron 2955U 1.4 GHz (Haswell micro-architecture), 2 DDR3L SDRAM, 32 GB Solid-State Drive, 11.6" anti-glare screen, Intel HD graphics, 1x USB 3.0, 1x USB 2.0, 1x HDMI™, full-size Acer FineTip keyboard, and multi-gesture touchpad.

The operating system, you may have guessed it, is Chrome OS. For current offers of the Acer C720-2420 in the US, check out this page on Amazon.com.
