Acer Chromebook Spin 312 CP312-1HN-34Z7 NX.J0PAA.001 Convertible Touchscreen Laptop

Operating System: Chrome OS

Touch Screen: Yes

Acer's Chromebook Spin 312 is a versatile, compact laptop designed with modern users in mind. Running on Chrome OS, this device provides a streamlined experience with fast boot times and a highly responsive performance, courtesy of the Intel Core i3-N305 processor. With a 12.2-inch WUXGA IPS touch display protected by Antimicrobial Corning Gorilla Glass, users can expect clear visuals whether working, watching, or creating.

This chromebook starts up in under ten seconds, with battery life lasting up to 13 hours, allowing you to handle work and leisure activities effectively without frequent recharging. The security and ease-of-use of Chrome OS are complemented by the built-in Titan C2 security chip, delivering peace of mind against viruses and malware.

Equipped with Intel Wi-Fi 6E connectivity, Bluetooth support, USB and HDMI ports, and a robust suite of Google applications, the Acer Chromebook Spin 312 ensures seamless multitasking. Additionally, its convertible design allows various usage modes, making it suitable for both professional presentations and compact travel.

Further enhancing productivity, the device is compatible with apps from Google Play like Microsoft 365, Minecraft, and more, supporting a variety of needs from creative tasks to productivity applications. Plus, users can benefit from a three-month free trial of the Google One AI Premium Plan, offering enhancements like increased storage and advanced tools.
