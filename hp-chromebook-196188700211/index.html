HP Chromebook Plus x360 Full HD Touchscreen Laptop

Brand: HP
Operating System: Chrome OS
Display: 14" FHD IPS (1920x1080)
Dimensions (WxDxH): 12.72" x 8.28" x 0.81"
Touch Screen: Yes
Weight: 3.03 lbs
Processor: 3.8 GHz Intel Core i3-N305
Memory: 8 GB LPDDR5
Storage: 256 GB SSD
WLAN: Wi-Fi 6E
Colors: Silver
Input Devices: Keyboard, Touchpad, Touchscreen, Webcam
Connectivity: Bluetooth, HDMI, USB-C, USB-A, Wi-Fi 6E

The HP Chromebook Plus x360 combines a sleek design with powerful hardware to cater to both casual users and productivity seekers. Here’s a look at what this Chromebook has to offer:

Display

This Chromebook features a 14-inch FHD IPS touchscreen with edge-to-edge bezels. The 1920x1080 resolution provides a vibrant and detailed visual experience, ideal for everything from watching shows to working on detailed presentations.

Performance

The HP Chromebook Plus x360 is powered by the Intel Core i3-N305 processor, an 8-core CPU with a turbo clock speed of up to 3.8 GHz. This strikes a balance between performance and energy efficiency, making it suitable for multitasking and light productivity tasks.

Memory and Storage

With 8GB of LPDDR5 RAM, you get faster and more energy-efficient performance than older memory standards. It also includes 416GB of combined storage (256GB PCIe SSD + 160GB removable storage via a docking station). The additional storage accessories—like the 128GB SSD and 32GB MicroSD—expand its capability for files, media, and backups.

Graphics and Webcam

The device is equipped with Intel UHD Graphics, delivering crisp visuals for everyday tasks and Full HD content. The HP True Vision FHD camera features a privacy shutter and ensures clear conferencing, even in low-light conditions.

Connectivity and Ports

Modern connectivity features include Wi-Fi 6E for faster networking and Bluetooth for wireless peripherals. Available ports consist of:

  • 2 x USB-C 3.0
  • 1 x USB-A 3.0
  • 1 x Headphone Jack

Battery and Fast Charge

While specific battery life details aren't provided, the HP Fast Charge feature allows you to reach 50% charge in approximately 45 minutes—a convenient feature for users on the go.

Other Notable Features

The Chromebook comes with Chrome OS, known for its simplicity and security. The device is lightweight at 3.03 lbs, and measures 12.72 x 8.28 x 0.81 inches, making it easily portable. The package also includes a 7-in-1 docking station and useful accessories such as a charging cable and cleaning cloth.

Verdict

The HP Chromebook Plus x360 is a versatile machine aimed at students, professionals, and anyone looking for a blend of portability and performance. While its specs are solid for everyday tasks, it's best suited for web-based workflows and light multitasking, thanks to the Chrome OS environment and Intel i3-N305 processor.
