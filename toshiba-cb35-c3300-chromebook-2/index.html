Toshiba CB35-C3300 Chromebook 2

Brand: Toshiba
Operating System: Chrome OS
Display: 13.3" HD Full IPS Display (1920x1080)
Dimensions (WxDxH): 12.6" x 8.4" x 0.76"
Weight: 2.9 lbs
Processor: 1.7 GHz Intel Celeron 3215U
Memory: 4 GB DDR3L SDRAM
Storage: 16 GB SSD
Battery life: 8.5 hours
WLAN: Wi-Fi 802.11 a/c
Colors: Silver
Input Devices: Keyboard, Touchpad, Microphone, Video Camera
Connectivity: USB, SD Card reader

The CB35-C3300 Chromebook is the lower-spec model of Toshiba's latest Chromebook released in September 2015. It is powered by an 1.7 GHz Intel Celeron 3215U dual-core processor, comes with 4 GB RAM and a 13.3-inch full HD IPS display.

Moreover, the CB35-C3300 is equipped with a 16 GB solid-state drive, 1 x USB 2.0 and 1 x USB 3.0 ports, HDMI port, SD card slot and built-in microphone and HD webcam, Kensington lock and Bluetooth 4.1.

The CB35-C3300 weighs only 2.9 pounds and is rated at 8.5 hours of battery life making it a solid device for mobile use. It was offered in the US for a start price of $329.99. For current offers, check out this product page on Amazon.com.

If you want a more powerful laptop with a core i3 processor, check out the Toshiba CB35-C3350 and the side-by-side comparison of the CB35-C3300 and CB35-C3350 Chromebooks.
