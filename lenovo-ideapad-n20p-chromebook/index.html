Lenovo IdeaPad N20p Chromebook

Brand: Lenovo
Operating System: Chrome OS
Display: 11.6" HD AntiGlare with Multitouch (1366x768)
Dimensions (WxDxH): 11.6" x 8.34" x 0.7"
Touch Screen: Yes
Weight: 2.87 lbs
Processor: 2.16 GHz Intel Celeron N2830
Memory: 2 GB DDR3
Storage: 16 GB SSD
Battery life: 8 hours
WLAN: Wi-Fi 802.11 a/c
Colors: Silver
Input Devices: Keyboard, Touchpad, Microphone, Video Camera
Connectivity: Bluetooth, USB, SD Card reader

The Lenovo IdeaPad N20p Chromebook is one of the few Chromebooks that has a touchscreen with multitouch capabilities and the only convertible one, that allows you to easily switch between laptop and stand mode, I'm aware of. The thin and lightweight laptop offers 8 hours of battery life and weighs a little less than 3 lbs.

The Lenovo IdeaPad N20p is powered by a dual-core Intel Celeron N2830 with a clock-rate of 2.16 GHz and comes with 2 GB RAM and 16 GB SSD. The laptop has Bluetooth 4.0 support, 1 USB 3.0 and 1 USB 2.0 ports, HDMI out and a 2-in-1 (SD / MMC) card reader. The Lenovo IdeaPad N20p was first released in June 2014.
