Dell Inspiron Mini 9: 8 GB SSD White

Brand: Dell
Operating System: Ubuntu Netbook Remix
Display: 8.9" Backlit LED (1024x600)
Dimensions (WxDxH): 9.13" x 6.77" x 1.07"
Weight: 2.28 lbs
Processor: 1.6 GHz Intel Atom N270
Memory: 1 GB DDR2 SDRAM (max: 1 GB)
Storage: 8 GB SSD
Battery life: 4 hours
WLAN: WiFi 802.11g
LAN: 10/100 Mbit Ethernet
Colors: White
Input Devices: Keyboard, Touchpad, Video Camera
Connectivity: USB, SD Card reader

This white Dell Inspiron Mini 9 with 8 GB SSD is available as a pre configured system on Amazon. It's equipped with an Intel Atom Processor N270 1.6 GHz, 1 GB DDR2 at 533MHz, 8 GB Solid State Drive, an external portable CD/DVD-RW drive, a glossy 8.9 inch backlit LED display, an integrated 0.3M Pixel webcam, Intel Graphics Media Accelerator (GMA) 950, a wireless 802.11g mini card and runs Ubuntu Linux version 8.04.1.

Due to its ease of use Ubuntu is one of the most popular Linux distributions. Ubuntu Netbook Remix is optimized for the restricted screen size of netbooks and built for accessing your favorite applications quickly.

Some major open source applications included are the Firefox Web browser, the E-mail client Thunderbird, Pidgin for instant messaging, the photo viewer F-Spot and the office suite OpenOffice.org.
