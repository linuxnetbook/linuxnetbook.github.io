The HP Chromebook 14a-ne0047nr is an affordable, compact choice for students, professionals, and budget-conscious users. Optimized for modern workflows and cloud-centric tasks, this device delivers fundamental features without over-complication.

Key Specifications at a Glance

Processor and Performance

Powered by the Intel Celeron N4120 quad-core processor, this Chromebook has a base frequency of 1.1 GHz and a burst frequency of up to 2.6 GHz. It provides sufficient speed and efficiency for multitasking with basic applications and browsing. Integrated Intel UHD Graphics 600 support light graphics work and video playback.

Memory and Storage

With 4GB LPDDR4x RAM running at 4266 MT/s, the system enables smooth multitasking. Storage is split between 64GB eMMC onboard and an included 128GB microSD card, expanding its utility for offline projects or multimedia storage.

Display and Build

The Chromebook's 14-inch HD display (1366 x 768 resolution) includes an anti-glare, micro-edge panel with 250 nits brightness and 45% NTSC color gamut, making it suitable for both indoor and casual workspaces. At 12.85 x 8.94 x 0.72 inches and weighing just 3.35 lbs, its portability complements its minimalist "Modern Grey" color scheme.

Connectivity and Ports

With modern connectivity options, the laptop features:

  • 1 USB-C port (5Gbps, USB Power Delivery, DisplayPort 1.4)
  • 2 USB-A ports (5Gbps)
  • 1 HDMI 1.4b port
  • A headphone/microphone jack Wireless options include Bluetooth 5.1 and Wi-Fi 802.11a/b/g/n/ac (dual-band 2x2).

Battery Life and Charging

You can expect up to 10 hours of battery life from the 2-cell, 47Wh Lithium-ion polymer battery, along with support for fast charging (50% in 45 minutes) via the 45W USB-C power adapter.

Additional Features

  • HP True Vision 720p webcam with built-in dual-array microphones for video calls
  • Chrome OS, offering access to the Google Play Store, built-in security, and cloud integration
  • Dual speakers
  • Includes Hubxcel's 6-in-1 accessory bundle to enhance usability

Ideal for Everyday Users

The HP Chromebook 14a is a competent choice for basic computing tasks. It fits users who prioritize a simple yet efficient Chromebook for web applications, online meetings, or basic productivity. Its value package covers essential needs without breaking the bank.
