Samsung Chromebook 4 XE310XBA-KA1US - 11.6-inch Laptop

Brand: SAMSUNG
Operating System: Chrome OS
Display: 11.6" LED (1366x768)
Dimensions (WxDxH): 11.33" x 7.96" x 0.66"
Weight: 2.6 lbs
Processor: 1.1 GHz Intel Celeron N4020
Memory: 4 GB LPDDR4 (max: 4 GB)
Storage: 32 GB eMMC
Battery life: 12.5 hours
WLAN: Gigabit Wi-Fi (802.11ac)
Colors: Platinum Titan
Input Devices: Keyboard, Touchpad, Webcam
Connectivity: Bluetooth, HDMI, USB, Wi-Fi

The Samsung Chromebook 4 (2021 Model) is designed for users seeking a compact, durable, and efficient device for everyday tasks. With an 11.6-inch LED display offering a resolution of 1366x768, this Chromebook strikes a balance between portability and functionality.

Key Hardware Specs

Powered by an Intel Celeron Processor N4020 (dual-core), the Chromebook includes 4GB of LPDDR4 RAM and 32GB of eMMC storage, suitable for light multitasking, web browsing, and basic office work. Its hardware is complemented by Intel UHD Graphics 600, ensuring smooth performance for non-intensive graphical tasks.

Designed for Portability and Durability

Weighing just 2.6 pounds and measuring 0.66 inches thin, the device is extremely portable. Despite its lightweight build, it boasts military-grade durability, allowing it to withstand drops and harsh conditions—making it an excellent choice for students or frequent travelers.

Connectivity and Battery Life

Connectivity options include Gigabit Wi-Fi (802.11ac) for ultra-fast internet, USB ports, HDMI support, and Bluetooth compatibility for seamless peripheral integration. The 12.5-hour battery life ensures uninterrupted use throughout the day.

Advanced Security with Chrome OS

Running Chrome OS, the Samsung Chromebook 4 integrates multiple layers of built-in virus protection and frequent automated updates to keep threats at bay. The cloud-oriented operating system is ideal for those deeply invested in Google's productivity ecosystem.

Conclusion

The Samsung Chromebook 4 is a budget-friendly, no-frills Chromebook that suits users looking for reliability in a lightweight and secure package. Its emphasis on durability, long battery life, and Chrome OS's simplicity make it a rugged companion for everyday productivity.
