Toshiba CB35-B3340 Chromebook 2

Brand: Toshiba
Operating System: Chrome OS
Display: 13.3" Full HD IPS screen (1920x1080)
Dimensions (WxDxH): 12.6" x 8.4" x 0.76"
Weight: 3 lbs
Processor: 2.16 GHz Intel Celeron N2840
Memory: 4 GB DDR3L SDRAM
Storage: 16 GB SSD
Battery life: 9 hours
WLAN: Wi-Fi 802.11 a/c
Colors: Silver
Input Devices: Keyboard, Touchpad, Microphone, Video Camera
Connectivity: USB, SD Card reader

The CB35-B3340 is Toshiba's highest-spec Chromebook released so far, most notably it features a full HD IPS screen with 1920 x 1080 pixel resolution and comes with 4 GB RAM installed.

The Toshiba CB35-B3340 Chromebook 2 is powered by a 2.16 GHz Intel Celeron N2840 dual-core processor and equipped with a 16 GB solid-state drive, 1 x USB 2.0 and 1 x USB 3.0 ports, HDMI port, SD card slot and built-in dual-array microphones and an HD webcam.

The Toshiba CB35-B3340 weighs only 3 pounds and is rated at 9 hours of battery life, 2 hours less than the lower-spec CB35-B3330 Chromebook. More power comes at a price, the list price is $299.99.

Certainly one of the more "expensive" Chromebooks, but still a pretty good deal for a laptop with these specs if you ask me. What do you think?
