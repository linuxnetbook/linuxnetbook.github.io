Toshiba CB30-A3120 Chromebook

Brand: Toshiba
Operating System: Chrome OS
Display: 13.3" HD TruBrite display (1366x768)
Dimensions (WxDxH): 12.9" x 8.9" x 0.8"
Weight: 3.3 lbs
Processor: 1.4 GHz Intel Celeron 2955U
Memory: 2 GB DDR3 SDRAM
Storage: 16 GB SSD
Battery life: 9 hours
WLAN: Wi-Fi 802.11 a/b/g/n
Colors: Sunray Silver
Input Devices: Keyboard, Touchpad, Microphone, Video Camera
Connectivity: USB, SD Card reader

The Toshiba CB30-A3120 Chromebook has a 13.3 inch screen with a maximum resolution of 1366x768 pixels and is equipped with a 1.4 GHz dual-core Intel Celeron 2955U processor, 2 GB RAM, 16 GB SSD, 2 USB 3.0 ports, HDMI port, security lock slot and a memory card reader, Bluetooth 4.0, dual-band 802.11 a/b/g/n Wi-Fi, and a 2 MP HD Webcam.

The Toshiba CB30-A3120 boasts 9 hours of battery life, which is quite an impressive value and certainly a selling point. Also with its 13.3" screen it meets the requirements to get real work done from a hardware perspective. When it comes to the software, it very much depends on your needs, whether Chrome OS offers enough. The Toshiba CB30-A3120 was originally offered for US $299.
