Quick Look at the Lenovo ThinkPad 11e Chromebook

This video gives first impressions of the upcoming Lenovo ThinkPad 11e Chromebook. Yes, another of the big players in the laptop market will release a Chromebook soon. What sets the ThinkPad 11e apart from all other Chromebooks so far, is its form factor. It will be available as a clamshell-style notebook and as a Yoga-style convertible, i. e. a laptop/tablet hybrid. Obviously the Yoga model will have a touchscreen, which is still a rarity among chromebooks.

The robust ThinkPad 11e is geared towards the education market. Pricing starts at $349 for the basic Chrome OS model and goes up to $699 for a Windows convertible. It is supposed to be available as of June 2014. For more info check out the article on Liliputing, that you can read without any haste.
