Asus Eee PC 901 with Xandros Linux Review

With about 14 minutes length this comprehensive video review demonstrates what you can do with the Asus Eee PC 901 running Xandros Linux: play games, watch videos, access the Internet.

The creator of the video jkkmobile has the impression that the linux version of the Asus Eee PC 901 does not run as fast as the WinXP version and argues that windows users may have some trouble getting used to the large icon based interface.
