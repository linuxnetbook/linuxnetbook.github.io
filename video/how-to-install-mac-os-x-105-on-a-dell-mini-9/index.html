How to install Mac OS X 10.5 on a Dell Mini 9

In this video tutorial the Too Smart Guys walk through the process of installing Mac OS X 10.5 on a pink Dell Mini 9 netbook. Personally, I don't see a point in installing Mac OS X on a netbook, that can be ordered with Ubuntu Netbook Remix.

On the other hand, if you are in the miserable situation of having the Windows model, this entertaining howto video and the linked resources in the Dell Mini 9 Hackintosh show notes will help you switch to an operating system, that is at least based on Unix.
