A Quick Look at the Acer AC700 Chromebook

The Acer AC700 Chromebook has been available for a while and finally we have the chance to watch a video with Nicole from Netbook News getting her hands on the 11.6 inch laptop.

It shouldn't come as a surprise that Acer's Chromebook is very similar to Samsung's 1st model with Google's laptop OS. The Acer AC700 Chromebook is slightly smaller, with a little less battery life and a cheaper price. Check out the video to get some impressions of it.
