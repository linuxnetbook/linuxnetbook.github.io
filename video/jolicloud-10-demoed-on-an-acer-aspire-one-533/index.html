Jolicloud 1.0 Demoed on an Acer Aspire One 533

As announced last week version 1.0 of Jolicloud is currently being rolled out to the users of the netbook operating system. Probably the most obvious change compared to the pre-final release is the new HTML5 based launcher.

But there is much more under the hood, for example automatic synchronization of the Jolicloud operating system between different hardware devices, cloud based application management, simple cloud based file management with support for various storage services, and more than 700 apps that can be accessed through the Jolicloud App Center.

Founding members and early adopters are first served with the new version, so it shouldn't be a surprise that Sascha from Netbook News already installed Jolicloud 1.0 on an Acer Aspire One 533 netbook and created this video demonstrating the new version and talking about his pretty positive first impressions.

Looking forward to do the upgrade myself and play around with Jolicloud 1.0.
