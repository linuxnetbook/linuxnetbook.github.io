Samsung Chromebook Plus Demo at CES 2017

In this video Robby Payne of Chrome Unboxed gets his hands on the much anticipated Samsung Chromebook Plus at CES 2017. He gives a quick overview of the device and its tech specs.

Here are a few highlights:

  • solid build quality and keyboard
  • 360° convertible device for use as laptop or tablet
  • the 3:2 aspect ratio feels better in portrait mode than 16:9, which is especially nice when running Android apps
  • comes with a stylus pen for taking notes, making sketches, capturing a part of the screen etc
  • Octane score of around 10,000

Watch out for more coverage on the Samsung Chromebook Plus and other Chrome OS devices by subscribing to the Chrome Unboxed YouTube channel.
