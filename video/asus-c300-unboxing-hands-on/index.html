ASUS Chromebook C300 Unboxing and Hands-On

Another video by Robby Payne of Chrome Unboxed where he unboxes and takes a first look at the new ASUS C300 Chromebook. In a few minutes Robby provides us with first impressions of the design/look and build quality of the ASUS device.

Just as other Chromebooks it boots quickly. Unlike many other laptops it has a matte screen, which is preferable when you use it outside a lot. Even though this is a video recording the display looks pretty sharp to me, but apparently Robby is rather disappointed and hopes for better Chromebook displays in the future.
