Kevin Purcell Reviews the Lenovo Thinkpad Yoga 11e Chromebook

This is a short review of the Lenovo Thinkpad Yoga 11e Chromebook done by Kevin Purcell of GottaBeMobile. Kevin evaluates the ThinkPad Yoga as the best Chromebook option for people who need a very durable device in a rugged environment. He calls it a very strong entry into the Chromebook market and a buy especially for educational settings. Below is a summary of the pros and cons.

Pros

  • Most rugged Chromebook, strong and durable built for use in schools.
  • USB 2.0 port can be used to charge devices even when laptop is in sleep mode.
  • Excellent keyboard and trackpad.
  • Flexibility and versatility: use it as a tablet, laptop or in stand mode.

Cons

  • Glossy and reflective screen.
  • Not the most powerful device with only 2 GB RAM.
Operating Systems: Chrome OS
