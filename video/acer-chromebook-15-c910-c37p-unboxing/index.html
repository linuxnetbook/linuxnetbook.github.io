Acer Chromebook 15 C910-C37P Unboxing

This is a short video of Robby Payne unboxing one of the latest Chromebooks out there, the Acer Chromebook 15 C910-C37P. Robby confirms that the C910-C37P has a full HD screen and he assumes that it is an IPS display, but can't guarantee that as a fact.

After a few minutes of getting his hands on the Acer 15 Robby obviously cannot say too much about it, but his first impressions are good and he plans to use it as his main laptop for the next few weeks and post a review later.
