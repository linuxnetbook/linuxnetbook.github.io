HP Chromebook 11 Compared to Acer C720

This video presents a side-by-side comparison of the HP Chromebook 11 and the Acer C720. The video author prefers the finish and the display of the HP Chromebook, but in terms of performance and battery life the Acer C720 wins.

The video ends abruptly while game play of an online version of Doom is demoed on the two Chromebooks. The game runs more smoothly on the C720, but to be fair it has 4 GB RAM and the HP 11 has 2 GB. Would be interesting to see the differences of two devices with the same amount of RAM.
