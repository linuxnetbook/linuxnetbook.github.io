Compaq Airlife 100 Smartbook at Mobile World Congress

In this video Chippy from Carrypad gets his hands on the Compaq Airlife 100 at Mobile World Congress 2010, which currently takes place in Barcelona, Spain.

The Compaq Airlife 100 smartbook has a 10" resistive multi-touch display and is powered by a 1 GHz Qualcomm Snapdragon processor. It comes with 16GB SSD built-in 3G and WiFi, weighs less than 1kg (~2.2lbs), and is said to offer up to 12 hours of battery life.

HP's first ARM-based smartbook runs Google's Android OS and will first be offered by Spanish Telco Telefónica, probably in conjunction with a 24-month contract. Hopefully, it will be available without a telecom contract as well.
