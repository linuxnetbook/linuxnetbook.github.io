Danny Stieben Reviews the Toshiba CB35 A3120 Chromebook

This is a video review of the Toshiba CB35 A3120 Chromebook done by Danny Stieben of makeuseof. The Toshiba CB35 is one of the few Chromebooks on the market with a 13.3 inch screen. Of those with a larger screen it is at the lower end of the price range, which indicates some sacrifices that were made, see the pros and cons below:

Pros

  • Low price for a 13.3 screen laptop.
  • Performance better than expected from a 1.4 GHz dual-core processor.

Cons

  • Low built quality, the used materials feel cheap.
  • Only a resolution of 1366 x 768 pixels despite 13.3 inch screen.

Due to the low built quality Danny recommends to look for another 13.3 Chromebook, which means either the 13.3" Samsung Chromebook 2 or the ASUS C300.
