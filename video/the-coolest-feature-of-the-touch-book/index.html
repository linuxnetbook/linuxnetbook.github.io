The Coolest Feature of the Touch Book

Gregoire Gentil, founder of Always Innovating, personally demonstrates the Touch Book, a device that combines netbook and touchscreen tablet. In the 1st video Gregoire very briefly shows the Touch Book user interface, how to detach the screen, some touch gestures, and the portrait mode feature to turn the tablet into an ebook reader.

In the 2nd video below Gregoire demonstrates, what he considers the coolest feature of the Touch Book. You can stick it to the door of your fridge, watch videos, and most importantly slam the door of your fridge without the Touch Book falling off.

Don't try this at home, as the warranty of the Touch Book probably does not cover the repair costs for your fridge.
