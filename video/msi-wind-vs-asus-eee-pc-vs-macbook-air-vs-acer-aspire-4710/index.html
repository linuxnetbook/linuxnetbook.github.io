MSI Wind vs ASUS Eee PC vs MacBook Air vs Acer Aspire 4710

Fon founder Martin Varsavsky explains why the MSI Wind is the only computer bundled to with the Fonera 2.0 WiFi router comparing the Wind to an ASUS Eee PC, an Acer Aspire 4710, and Apple's MacBook Air.

One of the things he does not like at all about the Eee PC is the hard to use keyboard. He does not name the ASUS model he used for his comparison, but it looks like one of the Eee PC 9xx series.

The Acer Aspire 4710 notebook offers good usability which comes at the price of less portability. While Martin likes Mac OS X in contrast to Windows Vista, he has not much love for the MacBook Air, which has only one pathetic USB port, no sound input, no Ethernet, and is very expensive compared to other notebooks.

The MSI Wind offers great portability, has a usable keyboard, enough ports for connecting to other devices and can have multiple operating systems installed, including Ubuntu Linux, making it the best option for the Fonera 2.0 bundle.
