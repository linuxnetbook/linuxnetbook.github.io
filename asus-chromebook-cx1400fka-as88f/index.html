ASUS Chromebook Flip CX1 CX1400FKA-AS88F Convertible Laptop

Brand: ASUS
Operating System: Chrome OS
Display: 14" NanoEdge 360-degree Touchscreen (1920x1080)
Dimensions (WxDxH): 12.85" x 9.01" x 0.73"
Touch Screen: Yes
Weight: 4.54 lbs
Processor: 3.3 GHz Intel Celeron N6000
Memory: 8 GB LPDDR4X
Storage: 128 GB eMMC
Battery life: 11 hours
WLAN: Wi-Fi 6
Colors: Transparent Silver
Input Devices: Keyboard, Touchpad, Touchscreen, Webcam
Connectivity: Bluetooth 5.2, HDMI, USB 3.2, Wi-Fi, Micro SD Card reader

The ASUS Chromebook Flip CX1 offers a versatile and adaptable computing experience. Equipped with an Intel Celeron N6000 4-Core Processor, it provides a balance between power efficiency and performance for smooth multitasking. The 14-inch NanoEdge display, which boasts a full HD resolution of 1920x1080, is flippable and touchscreen-enabled, allowing users to work comfortably in multiple modes - clamshell, tent, stand, and tablet.

This Chromebook's lightweight build adheres to the US Military Grade standard (MIL-STD 810H), ensuring durability without adding extra weight, at just 3.59 lbs. It comes with 8GB RAM and 128GB eMMC storage, offering a robust environment for both work and play. Connectivity features include Wi-Fi 6, Bluetooth 5.2, multiple USB 3.2 ports, and a micro SD card reader, ensuring a seamless connection with peripheral devices.

The Chromebook Flip CX1 promises up to 11-hour battery life, making it a reliable choice for a day on the go. It runs on ChromeOS, providing access to Google apps and the flexibility to edit, download, and convert Microsoft Office files. Additionally, the device includes a 12-month Google One trial for expansive storage options.

This Chromebook is a suitable option for users seeking adaptability in their laptops, supported by ChromeOS efficiency and Google's broad suite of applications.
