Acer Aspire One

Brand: Acer
Operating System: Linpus Linux Lite
Display: 8.9" LED Backlit LCD (1024x600)
Dimensions (WxDxH): 9.8" x 6.7" x 1.1"
Weight: 2.2 lbs
Processor: 1.6 GHz Intel Atom N270
Memory: 512 MB SDRAM (max: 1 GB)
Storage: 8 GB SSD
Battery life: 2.5 hours
WLAN: 802.11b/g Wireless LAN
LAN: 10/100 Mbit Ethernet
Colors: Seashell White, Sapphire Blue, Coral Pink, Golden Brown
Input Devices: Keyboard, Touchpad, Microphone, Video Camera
Connectivity: USB, SD Card reader

The Aspire One is a small notebook by Taiwanese electronics manufacturer Acer Incorporated. The netbook has a 8.9" CrystalBrite LED Backlit LCD screen, a 1.6 GHz Intel Atom N270 processor, 512 GB RAM, 8 GB of flash-based solid state storage, and a 3-cell battery. The One is also available with a 2.5" 120 GB hard disk drive.

The Acer Aspire One was first released in July 2008 and is one of the most popular mini notebooks currently available, because of its comparably low price, a high-quality keyboard, a bright screen, and the good build quality.

The Linux version of the "One" comes pre-installed with the Linpus Lite Linux distribution which is based on Fedora 8. Linpus Lite offers a simplified user interface and features applications like the Mozilla Firefox Web browser and the free office package OpenOffice.org.
