HP Chromebook 11A G8 Education Edition 16W64UT#ABA-CR

Brand: HP
Operating System: Chrome OS
Display: 11.6" Anti-Glare Display (1366x768)
Dimensions (WxDxH): 9.1" x 9.1" x 9.1"
Weight: 1.0 lbs
Processor: 1.6 GHz AMD A4-9120C APU
Memory: 4 GB DDR4 SDRAM

WLAN: Qualcomm Wi-Fi 5

Input Devices: Keyboard, Touchpad, Webcam, Integrated microphone
Connectivity: Bluetooth, HDMI, USB, Wi-Fi

The HP Chromebook 11A G8 Education Edition is a versatile option for users looking for a simple and durable computing solution. Equipped with an AMD A4-9120C APU, this Chromebook features a dual-core processor with a base clock speed of 1.6 GHz and up to 2.4 GHz max boost, providing adequate performance for basic tasks. It runs on Chrome OS, offering a user-friendly experience with seamless access to web-based applications.

Sporting a compact 11.6-inch HD display with a resolution of 1366 x 768, this Chromebook is designed for easy portability with its lightweight at just 1 kg. The anti-glare screen with 220 nits brightness ensures comfort during prolonged use, although it may not provide the best outdoor visibility.

With 4GB DDR4-1866 SDRAM and 32GB of eMMC storage, the HP Chromebook 11A G8 provides sufficient memory and storage for general purposes. Connectivity options include Wi-Fi 5, Bluetooth 4.2, and several ports including 2 USB 3.1 Type-C, 2 USB 2.0, a microSD slot, and a stereo headphone/microphone combo jack. The additional HDMI support is absent, leaving reliance on available USB Type-C ports for display outputs.

The Chromebook's input features include a full-size, pick and spill-resistant keyboard, a responsive touchpad with multi-touch gesture support, a 720p HD webcam, and integrated microphone, facilitating reliable audio and video communication for students or remote workers. The HP 2-cell, 47 Wh Li-ion Polymer battery ensures a decent power backup, complementing its use in educational or casual settings.
