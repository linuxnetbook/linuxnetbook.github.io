CTL Chromebook J2

Brand: CTL
Operating System: Chrome OS
Display: 11.6" HD LED Anti-Glare screen (1366x768)
Dimensions (WxDxH): 11.42" x 8.03" x 0.76"
Weight: 2.54 lbs
Processor: 1.8 GHz Rockchip RK3288
Memory: 2 GB DDR3L SDRAM
Storage: 16 GB eMMC
Battery life: 9 hours
WLAN: Wi-Fi 802.11 a/c
Colors: Black
Input Devices: Keyboard, Touchpad, Microphone, Video Camera
Connectivity: Bluetooth, USB, SD Card reader

The CTL Chromebook J2 for Education is a powerful low-price laptop for students and teachers. It has a rugged design, an 11.6-inch anti-glare display, is powered by a quad-core Rockchip RK3288-C processor, and equipped with 2 GB RAM and 16 GB eMMC storage.

The small lightweight device easily fits into many backpacks or book bags and the textured cover makes it less likely to slip when carried around in the hands. Of special interest for educational settings are the service and support offerings stated on the manufacturer page:

The CTL Chromebook J2 is available with a 3-year warranty with accidental damage coverage. CTL Chromebook J2 models will include one year of Securly content filtering and analysis, allowing schools and parents to easily set automatic website and app filtering policies. In addition, it can be bundled for educators with Chrome Device Management licenses, Hapara license, and Pearson Education Software and eTextbooks.

The CTL Chromebook J2 was released for sale in July 2015 with a recommended retail price of US $199.
