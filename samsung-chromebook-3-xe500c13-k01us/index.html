Samsung Chromebook 3 XE500C13 K01

Brand: Samsung
Operating System: Chrome OS
Display: 11.6" HD LED Display (1366x768)
Dimensions (WxDxH): 11.37" x 8.04" x 0.7"
Weight: 2.54 lbs
Processor: 1.6 GHz Intel Celeron N3050
Memory: 2 GB LPDDR3
Storage: 16 GB eMMC
Battery life: 11 hours
WLAN: Wi-Fi 802.11 a/c
Colors: Metallic black
Input Devices: Keyboard, Touchpad, Microphone, Video Camera
Connectivity: Bluetooth, USB

The Samsung Chromebook 3 XE500C13 K01 is one of Samsung's 3rd generation Chrome OS laptops. It sports a 11.6-inch display, is equipped with a 1.6 GHz (up to 2.16 GHz with Turbo Boost) dual-core Intel Celeron N3050 processor, 2 GB RAM and 16 GB eMMC storage.

The slim and lightweight Chrome OS laptop offers fast Wi-Fi 802.11 a/c speeds and up to 11 hours of battery life. It is well suited for mobile use and tasks, that do not demand a lot of computing power, such as communication, reading, listening to music or watching videos.

The Samsung Chromebook 3 XE500C13 K01 was released for sale in February 2016 for an initial price of US $189.
