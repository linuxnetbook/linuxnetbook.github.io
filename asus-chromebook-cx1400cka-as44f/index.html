ASUS Chromebook CX1 CX1400CKA-AS44F

Brand: ASUS
Operating System: Chrome OS
Display: 14" FHD (1920x1080)
Dimensions (WxDxH): 12.85" x 9.0" x 0.74"
Weight: 2.87 lbs
Processor: 2.8 GHz Intel Celeron N4500
Memory: 4 GB LPDDR4X
Storage: 64 GB eMMC
Battery life: 12 hours
WLAN: WiFi 6
Colors: Transparent Silver
Input Devices: 1x audio jack, 2x USB 3.2 Gen 1 Type-C, 2x USB 3.2 Gen 1 Type-A, Micro SD Card reader
Connectivity: WiFi 6, Bluetooth 5.2

The ASUS Chromebook CX1 is a stylish and ultraportable laptop designed for individuals who value performance and mobility. With its Intel Celeron N4500 Processor, this Chromebook ensures smooth and efficient processing power, perfect for everyday tasks.

Key Features:

  • Display: Enjoy the 14-inch FHD (1920 x 1080) display, offering crystal-clear visuals for both work and entertainment.
  • Durability: Built to last with its US Military grade standard MIL-STD 810H certification, making it ideal for a robust lifestyle.
  • Ergonomics: Features a 180-degree lay-flat hinge and 1.4mm key travel for comfortable typing and flexible working angles.
  • Portability: Weighing only 2.87 lbs, this Chromebook is easy to carry around, fitting perfectly into your dynamic lifestyle.
  • Battery Life: With up to 12 hours of battery life, stay productive throughout your day without constantly searching for a power outlet.
  • Connectivity: Fast and stable with WiFi 6 and Bluetooth 5.2, ensuring seamless connectivity wherever you are.
  • Storage & RAM: Comes equipped with 64GB of fast eMMC storage and 4GB RAM, supporting smooth multitasking.
  • Sustainability: Sustainably manufactured with EPEAT Silver certification, contributing to a greener planet.
  • Ports: Includes 1x audio jack, 2x USB 3.2 Gen 1 Type-C (with display/power delivery support), 2x USB 3.2 Gen 1 Type-A, and a Micro SD Card reader for expanded functionality.
