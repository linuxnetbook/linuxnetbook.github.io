Where to Buy a Used or Refurbished Chromebook in the US?

Most Chromebooks are at the lower end of the price spectrum for laptops. Several models like the Haier or the Sector 5 E1 Chromebooks cost less than $200 new. If you need more power, but don't want to pay more, a used or refurbished Chromebook is a sensible option. Below you find places for US based customers to buy used Chromebooks.

Amazon

Amazon has a wide range of Chromebooks that are offered both as new or used devices. If you know which Chromebook model you want, you can simply go to the corresponding product page and check out the used offers. If you're not decided on the model, you can do a search for Refurbished Chromebook or if it makes you feel better Certified Refurbished Chromebook.

eBay

eBay is another huge marketplace, where you can find new and used Chromebooks. The strategy is more or less the same as on Amazon, either search for a particular model or start with one of the listings below to see the available offers:

  • Used Chrome OS Laptops
  • Certified Refurbished Chrome OS Laptops
  • Seller Refurbished Chrome OS Laptops

Craigslist

Craigslist is another WWW dinosaur with a large user base, that is worth checking out. Start from the appropriate location in the US and then simply search for Chromebook using the site search.

Other places

From my experience you typically find the lowest price offers on one of the sites above, but there are other options. Brands like Dell or HP have their own online stores, where they offer refurbished products. Moreover, you can check listings in relevant papers and magazines or local bulletin boards.
