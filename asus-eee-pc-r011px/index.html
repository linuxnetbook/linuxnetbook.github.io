ASUS Eee PC R011PX

Brand: ASUS
Operating System: Ubuntu
Display: 10.1" (1024x600)
Dimensions (WxDxH): 10.3" x 7" x 1.4"
Weight: 2.75 lbs
Processor: 1.66 GHz Intel Atom N455
Memory: 2 GB DDR3 SDRAM (max: 2 GB)
Storage: 320 GB HDD
Battery life: 4 hours
WLAN: WiFi 802.11b/g/n
LAN: 10/100 Mbit Ethernet
Colors: Black
Input Devices: Keyboard, Touchpad, Microphone, Video Camera
Connectivity: USB, SD Card reader

The ASUS Eee PC R011PX is a 10.1 inch netbook powered by a 1.66GHz Intel Atom N455 processor that is available with Ubuntu Linux version 10.10 in Germany.

The netbook has a 10.1" LED backlight anti glare display with 1024x600 maximum resolution, comes with 2GB RAM, a 320GB hard disk drive, an integrated SD/SDHC/MMC card reader, a built-in 0.3 megapixel webcam, 3 x USB 2.0 and 1 x VGA port, wireless networking 802.11b/g/n and a three cell battery up to four hours of operation.
