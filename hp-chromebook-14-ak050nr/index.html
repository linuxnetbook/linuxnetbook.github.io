HP Chromebook 14-ak050nr

Brand: HP
Operating System: Chrome OS
Display: 14" Full HD IPS WLED-backlit display (1920x1080)
Dimensions (WxDxH): 13.54" x 9.45" x 0.7"
Weight: 3.74 lbs
Processor: 1.83 GHz Intel Celeron N2940
Memory: 4 GB DDR3L SDRAM
Storage: 16 GB eMMC
Battery life: 9.25 hours
WLAN: Wi-Fi 802.11 ac
Colors: Snow White, Turbo Silver
Input Devices: Keyboard, Touchpad, Microphone, Video Camera
Connectivity: USB, SD Card reader

The HP Chromebook 14-ak050nr is a Chrome OS laptop with 14-inch full-HD IPS WLED-backlit display. It is powered by a 1.83 GHz quad-core Intel Celeron N2940 processor.

This laptop is equipped with 4GB RAM, 16GB eMMC storage, a HP TrueVision HD Webcam (720p), built-in microphone, 3 USB (1x3.0 + 2x2.0) ports, 1 multi-format SD media card reader, a full-size island-style keyboard and a battery that lasts up to 9 hours and 15 minutes according to HP. The HP Chromebook 14-ak050nr was released in early November 2015 and offered for a start price of $279.99.
