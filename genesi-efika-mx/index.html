Genesi EFIKA MX

Brand: Genesi
Operating System: Ubuntu
Display: 10.1" (1024x600)
Dimensions (WxDxH): 10.87" x 7.13" x 0.83"
Weight: 2.05 lbs
Processor: 0.8 GHz Freescale i.MX515
Memory: 512 MB DDR2 RAM
Storage: 16 GB SSD
WLAN: WiFi 802.11 b/g/n
Colors: Black
Input Devices: Keyboard, Touchpad, Microphone, Video Camera
Connectivity: Bluetooth, USB, SD Card reader, microSD

The EFIKA MX Smartbook by Genesi is a small and light portable computer targeted at developers and experienced users. It has a 10.1" LED backlight TFT-LCD screen and is powered by a 800MHz Freescale i.MX515 processor.

The EFIKA MX is equippd with 512MB RAM, a 16GB Nand Flash drive, a 3D graphics processing unit, multi-format HD video decoder and D1 video encoder, built-in 1.3MP video camera, microphone, and stereo speakers, 2 x USB 2.0 ports, internal MicroSD slot, a 3‐cell battery and worldwide AC adapter, and features support for 802.11 b/g/n WiFi and Bluetooth 2.1 + EDR.

The EFIKA Smartbook ships with a derivative of Ubuntu Maverick (10.10) pre-installed with an optimized Linux kernel version 2.6.31.14. It is currently offered for US $199 in the United States (page was removed) and EUR 189.00 in the European Union (page was removed), EU customers may have expect 4 weeks of shipping time.
