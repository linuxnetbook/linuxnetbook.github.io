Lenovo ThinkPad X131e Chromebook 628323U

Brand: Lenovo
Operating System: Chrome OS
Display: 11.6" HD LED Anti-Glare (1366x768)
Weight: 3.9 lbs
Processor: 1.5 GHz Intel Celeron 1007U
Memory: 4 GB DDR
Storage: 16 GB SSD
Battery life: 8.5 hours
WLAN: Wi-Fi 802.11 a/b/g/n
Colors: Midnight Black
Input Devices: Keyboard, Touchpad, Microphone, Video Camera
Connectivity: Bluetooth, USB, SD Card reader

The ThinkPad X131e is Lenovo's first Chromebook made available to schools in January 2013. The laptop with the rugged chassis is built to stand the daily rigors of K-12 education. As most Chromebooks it has a 11.6" screen but is heavier than most models at 3.9 lbs.

The ThinkPad X131e is powered by a 1.5 GHz dual-core Intel Celeron processor, comes with 4 GB RAM (upgradeable to 8 GB), 16 GB SSD, a 720p Webcam, 2x USB 3.0, 1x USB 2.0, HDMI and VGA ports.

Being a somewhat special device in the Chromebook universe, this model costs more than most other Chromebooks with similar specs.
