ASUS Eee PC 4G

Brand: ASUS
Operating System: Xandros
Display: 7" TFT LCD (800x480)
Dimensions (WxDxH): 8.9" x 6.3" x 1.3"
Weight: 2 lbs
Processor: 0.9 GHz Intel Celeron
Memory: 512 MB DDR2 SDRAM
Storage: 4 GB SSD
Battery life: 3.5 hours
WLAN: 802.11b/g Wireless LAN
LAN: 10/100 Mbit Ethernet
Colors: Galaxy Black, Blush Pink, Pearl White
Input Devices: Keyboard, Touchpad, Video Camera
Connectivity: USB, SD Card reader

With dimensions of 8.9" x 6.3" x 1.3" (22.5 cm x 16.5 cm x 3.5 cm) and a weight of merely 2 lb (0.92 kg) the ASUS Eee PC 4G is one of the smallest netbooks available. This compact and highly portable device has a 7" wide color TFT LCD screen with a maximum resolution of 800 x 480-pixel (WXGA).

The Eeee 4G is powered by an Intel Celeron M ULV 900MHz processor. It comes with 512 MB DDR2 RAM, 4 GB Solid State Drive, an integrated 10/100 network card for fast ethernet connectivity, integrated wireless LAN, 1 x SD card slot, 3 x USB 2.0 ports, 1 VGA monitor out, headphone jack, microphone input and a 0.3 megapixel Webcam. The 4-cell 5200 mAh 7.4V Li-Ion battery is rated at 3.5 hours.

The pre-installed operating system Xandros Linux features applications like the Firefox Web browser and the office suite OpenOffice.org that enable you to accomplish many common tasks away from home and office.
