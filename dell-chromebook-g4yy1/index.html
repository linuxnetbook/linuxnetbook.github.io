Dell Chromebook 3100 G4YY1

Brand: Dell
Operating System: Chrome OS
Display: 11.6" (1366x768)
Dimensions (WxDxH): 8.2" x 12.0" x 0.82"
Weight: 2.84 lbs
Processor: 1.1 GHz Intel Celeron N4020
Memory: 4 GB
Storage: 32 GB GB eMMC
Battery life: 14 hours
Colors: Black
Input Devices: Keyboard, Touchpad, Webcam
Connectivity: Bluetooth, HDMI, USB, Wi-Fi

The Dell Chromebook 3100 is a compact, lightweight device designed for on-the-go productivity. Featuring an 11.6-inch display, it is equipped with an Intel Celeron N4020 processor and comes with 4 GB of RAM and a 32 GB eMMC storage.

Key Features

  • Build and Design: The laptop is housed in a sleek black chassis, measuring 8.2 inches in width, 12 inches in depth, and 0.82 inches in height, with a weight of 2.84 pounds, making it easily portable.
  • Connectivity: Users can take advantage of various connectivity options, including Bluetooth, HDMI, USB ports, and Wi-Fi, ensuring versatile connections for external devices and networks.
  • Input Devices: The Chromebook includes a standard keyboard, a touchpad, and a built-in webcam, serving the needs of students and professionals alike.
  • Operating System: The device runs on Chrome OS, providing a user-friendly experience with access to a wide range of applications from the Google ecosystem.

Conclusion

With its reasonable specifications and durable design, the Dell Chromebook 3100 is a reliable option for users looking for a functional laptop for basic tasks such as browsing, document editing, and streaming. Its affordability and lightweight nature further enhance its appeal as a practical choice for educational and casual use.
