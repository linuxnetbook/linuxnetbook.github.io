Samsung Galaxy Chromebook 4 XE310XBA-K02US

Brand: Samsung
Operating System: Chrome OS
Display: 11.6" LED (1366x768)
Dimensions (WxDxH): 11.33" x 7.96" x 0.66"
Weight: 2.98 lbs
Processor: 1.1 GHz Intel Celeron N4000
Memory: 4 GB DDR4
Storage: 64 GB eMMC
Battery life: 12.5 hours
WLAN: Gigabit Wi-Fi
Colors: Silver
Input Devices: Keyboard, Touchpad, Webcam
Connectivity: Bluetooth, HDMI, USB, Wi-Fi

The Samsung Galaxy Chromebook 4 (Model XE310XBA-K02US) is a compact and durable laptop designed for everyday use. Running Chrome OS, this device is an excellent choice for users who prioritize portability, security, and reliable performance for basic tasks.

Design and Build

This Chromebook features an 11.6-inch LED display with a resolution of 1366x768, offering an optimal size for work and play. Its sleek silver exterior, combined with military-grade durability (MIL-STD-810G), ensures it can endure minor drops, bumps, and harsh environmental conditions.

Performance

Powered by an Intel Celeron Processor N4000 and 4GB of DDR4 RAM, the Galaxy Chromebook 4 is well-suited for web browsing, streaming, and productivity apps. The 64GB eMMC storage provides enough space for documents, apps, and media, while ultra-fast Gigabit Wi-Fi connectivity ensures seamless online experiences.

Battery Life

A standout feature of this device is its impressive 12.5-hour battery life, allowing you to use the laptop throughout the day without constant recharging. The USB-C charging port supports fast charging, minimizing downtime.

Security and Features

With built-in multiple layers of virus protection, the Galaxy Chromebook 4 offers robust security out of the box. It also supports Google Assistant for voice commands, adding convenience for hands-free operations.

Input and Connectivity

The device includes a full-sized keyboard and spacious touchpad, alongside a built-in webcam for video calls. Connectivity options include Bluetooth, USB, HDMI, and Wi-Fi, making it versatile for various peripherals and external displays.

Summary

Released in October 2019, the Samsung Galaxy Chromebook 4 is light (2.98 lbs), slim (0.66 inches thick), and easy to carry, making it a practical option for students, travelers, or casual users. While it lacks advanced specs like a touchscreen or high-resolution display, its affordability, simplicity, and all-day battery life make it an excellent value for basic computing needs.
