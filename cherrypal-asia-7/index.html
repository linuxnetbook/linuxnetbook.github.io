Cherrypal Asia 7-inch

Brand: Cherrypal
Operating System: Android
Display: 7" (800x480)
Dimensions (WxDxH): 8.4" x 5.58" x 1.21"
Weight: 2 lbs
Processor: 0.533 GHz VIA 8505
Memory: 256 MB DDR2
Storage: 2 GB SSD
WLAN: WiFi 802.11 b/g
LAN: 10/100 Ethernet
Colors: Black
Input Devices: Keyboard, Touchpad, Microphone
Connectivity: USB, SD Card reader

The Cherrypal Asia comes in 2 flavors a 7" and a 10" model. The 7" netbook is equipped with an 533MHz ARM9 based VIA 8505 processor, 256MB DDR RAM, 2 GB NAND-flash drive, 10/100M Ethernet, WiFi 802.11 b/g, a QWERTY 86 keys keyboard, touchpad, built-in speaker and microphone, 1 x USB 2.0 and 2 x USB 1.1 ports, SD card , U-Disk, USB-HDD card port, and a 7.4 V 2100Mha built in Lithium battery.

The Cherrypal Asia netbook is the successor to the Cherrypal Africa announced in December 2010. Actually, it replaces the Africa model, which is no more available in Cherrypal's store.

The Cherrypal Asia runs Android version 1.6 and is available from the Cherrypal online store for an initial price of USD $99.00 plus shipping costs.
