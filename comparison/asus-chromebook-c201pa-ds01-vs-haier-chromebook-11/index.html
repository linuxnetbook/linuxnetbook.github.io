ASUS Chromebook C201PA-DS01 vs. Haier Chromebook 11

ASUS Chromebook C201PA-DS01

The ASUS Chromebook C201PA-DS01 is an 11.6-inch laptop powered by a 1.8GHz quad-core Rockchip 3288-C processor. It comes with 2GB RAM and 16 GB eMMC.

Haier Chromebook 11

The Haier Chromebook is a very cheap laptop with a 11.6-inch screen powered by a quad-core Rockchip RK3288 Coretex-A17 processor clocked at 1.8 GHz.
