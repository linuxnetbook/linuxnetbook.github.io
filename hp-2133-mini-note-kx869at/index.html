HP 2133 Mini-Note KX869AT

Brand: HP
Operating System: SUSE Linux Enterprise Desktop
Display: 8.9" WXGA (1280x768)
Dimensions (WxDxH): 10" x 6.5" x 1"
Weight: 2.8 lbs
Processor: 1.2 GHz VIA C7-M ULV
Memory: 1 GB DDR2 SDRAM (max: 2 GB)
Storage: 120 GB HDD
Battery life: 2.25 hours
WLAN: WiFi 802.11a/b/g
LAN: 10/100/1000 Ethernet
Input Devices: Keyboard, Touchpad, Microphone, Video Camera
Connectivity: USB, SD Card reader, Expresscard 54 slot

The HP 2133 Mini-Note is a full-function personal computer equipped with a 1.2GHz VIA C7-M ULV (ultra-low voltage) processor, 1024 MB RAM, 120 GB (5400RPM) hard disk drive, 2 USB 2.0 ports, 1 ExpressCard/54 and 1 SD card slot, 1 headphone/line-out, 1 microphone port, and 1 VGA monitor port, 10/100/1000 Ethernet, and tri-mode Wi-Fi connectivity (802.11a/b/g).

With a 8.9" scratch-resistant WXGA resolution (1280x768) display, a nearly full-size keyboard (92%), built-in VGA webcam, a weight of 2.8 pounds, and up to 2.25 hours of battery life the HP 2133 Mini is well suited for net-centric mobile computing tasks.

The HP 2133 is powered by Novell's SUSE Linux Enterprise Desktop 10, that offers an easy-to-learn user interface, handles enterprise-grade business tools, and can peacefully coexist with Windows. It connects to Microsoft Active Directory, GroupWise, Lotus Domino, and Microsoft Exchange servers.

Installed applications include OpenOffice.org, Evolution Email, Evince Document Viewer, F-Spot Photo Browser, Gnome CD/DVD Creator, Helix Banshee Music Player, Real Player 10 media player, Firefox Web browser, Gaim instant messenger, Gnome FTP file transfer, and Liferea RSS feed reader.
