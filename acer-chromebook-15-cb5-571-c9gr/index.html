Acer Chromebook 15 CB5-571 C9GR

Brand: Acer
Operating System: Chrome OS
Display: 15.6" Full-HD IPS LED-backlit Display (1920x1080)
Dimensions (WxDxH): 15.04" x 10.08" x 0.95"
Weight: 4.85 lbs
Processor: 1.5 GHz Intel Celeron 3205U
Memory: 4 GB DDR3L SDRAM
Storage: 32 GB SSD
Battery life: 8 hours
WLAN: Wi-Fi 802.11 a/c
Colors: Moonstone White
Input Devices: Keyboard, Touchpad, Microphone, Video Camera
Connectivity: USB, SD Card reader

The Acer Chromebook 15 CB5-571 sports a 15.6-inch screen, the most popular laptop screen size by far. The CB5-571 C9GR model is powered by a 1.5 GHz dual-core Intel Celeron 3205U processor, comes with 4 GB RAM, 32 GB SSD, 1x USB 2.0 and 1x USB 3.0 ports, HDMI port, Wi-Fi 802.11 a/c, Bluetooth 4.0, a 1.3MP webcam and offers up to 8 hours of battery life.

The Acer Chromebook 15 CB5-571 C9GR was available for an initial price of €379 in Germany, making it one of the more expensive Chromebooks. Do you think Chromebooks with better hardware and therefore higher price points will be a success?
