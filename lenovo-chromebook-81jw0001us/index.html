Lenovo Chromebook S330 81JW0001US Laptop

Brand: Lenovo
Operating System: Chrome OS
Display: 14" HD Anti-Glare (1366x768)
Dimensions (WxDxH): 17.3" x 11.6" x 2.7"
Weight: 1.5 lbs
Processor: 1.7 GHz MediaTek MTK8173C
Memory: 4 GB LPDDR3 1866 MHz
Storage: 32 GB eMMC
WLAN: Wi-Fi 802.11
Colors: Black
Input Devices: Keyboard, Touchpad, Webcam, Microphone
Connectivity: Bluetooth 4.1, HDMI, USB 3.0, USB Type-C, Wi-Fi, SD Card Reader, Headphone/Microphone Combo Jack, Kensington Lock Slot

The Lenovo Chromebook S330 is a budget-friendly, lightweight device designed primarily for web browsing, media consumption, and productivity tasks. Running on Chrome OS, this Chromebook is perfect for users seeking a fast and streamlined computing experience.

Key Features

Performance and Hardware

Powered by a MediaTek MTK8173C processor clocked at up to 1.70 GHz with 4 cores, the S330 offers sufficient performance for everyday tasks. It comes with 4 GB of LPDDR3 1866 MHz RAM and 32 GB of eMMC storage, suitable for storing documents, apps, and essential files. However, its storage is limited compared to traditional laptops, so cloud storage is a key part of its design.

Display and Graphics

The 14-inch HD (1366 x 768) anti-glare display delivers decent visuals, and the integrated MediaTek graphics provide adequate performance for media playback and light tasks. It's not a touchscreen model, which may be a consideration if touch input is important to you.

Connectivity and Input

The Chromebook comes with a range of connectivity options:

  • 1 USB 3.0 port, 1 USB Type-C port
  • Bluetooth 4.1 and Wi-Fi 802.11
  • HDMI output for connecting to external displays
  • SD Card Reader for expandable storage
  • Headphone/microphone combo jack
  • A Kensington Lock Slot for added security

Input devices include a keyboard, touchpad, and a 720p HD webcam with an integrated microphone, making it suitable for video calls.

Audio and Battery

The dual 2W speakers provide respectable audio quality for its size. The battery is a 2-cell Lithium-ion unit, though the actual battery life isn't specified in the specs. Users can typically expect several hours of usage for standard Chrome OS devices like this one.

Build and Dimensions

Weighing in at just 1.5 kg (3.3 lbs) and measuring 17.3 cm x 11.6 cm x 2.7 cm, the S330 is slim and portable. Its black finish gives it a professional and understated look.

Ideal Use Cases

Although the Lenovo Chromebook S330 has limited hardware compared to high-end laptops, it excels in simplicity and speed. It is well-suited for students, casual users, or as a secondary device for lightweight tasks.

Conclusion

The Lenovo Chromebook S330 offers great value for those seeking a low-cost, no-frills Chromebook. It's best for users who require a straightforward device for internet browsing, streaming, and working within the Google ecosystem. However, the limited storage and lack of advanced features, such as a touchscreen or high-resolution display, may not meet the needs of power users.
