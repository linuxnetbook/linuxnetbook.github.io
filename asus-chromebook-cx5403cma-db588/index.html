ASUS ExpertBook CX54 Chromebook Plus - Laptop with Google AI

Brand: ASUS
Operating System: Chrome OS
Display: 14" WUXGA anti-glare (2560x1600)
Dimensions (WxDxH): 12.35" x 8.77" x 0.67"
Weight: 2.87 lbs
Processor: 1.2 GHz Intel Core Ultra 5 Processor
Memory: 8 GB LPDDR4x
Storage: 128 GB SSD
WLAN: Wi-Fi 6
Colors: Fog Silver
Input Devices: Keyboard, Touchpad, Webcam, Fingerprint Sensor
Connectivity: Bluetooth, HDMI, USB, Wi-Fi

The ASUS ExpertBook CX5403CMA-DB588 is a Chromebook designed for users seeking powerful AI features and a high-quality build. Running ChromeOS, this laptop integrates advanced Google AI tools for creative productivity, offering features that enhance writing, photo editing, and video calls.

Key Features

  • AI-Powered Applications: The Chromebook includes 12 months of the Google One AI Premium plan at no extra cost, granting access to features such as Magic Eraser, advanced noise cancelation, video call enhancements, and Gemini AI in Gmail and Docs.
  • Performance and Storage: Equipped with an Intel Core Ultra 5 Processor, 8GB of LPDDR4x RAM, and a 128GB SSD, this Chromebook efficiently handles multitasking and file storage needs.
  • 14-Inch High-Resolution Display: The WUXGA 2560x1600 anti-glare display offers a vibrant and sharp viewing experience, suitable for productivity and entertainment.
  • Built for Mobility: Weighing only 2.87 lbs and featuring a thin aluminum chassis, this Chromebook is durable and easy to carry.
  • Fingerprint Sensor: A built-in fingerprint sensor simplifies secure logins, providing fast and reliable access.
  • Connectivity and Battery: Equipped with Wi-Fi 6, multiple USB ports, and an HDMI port, it ensures solid connectivity. Battery life details were unspecified at the time of release but are optimized for all-day use based on similar models.

Durability and Build

Designed with a premium military-grade aluminum body, the ASUS ExpertBook CX54 is built to withstand the rigors of daily use while maintaining a polished and professional appearance. Its compact size (12.35 x 8.77 x 0.67 inches) and lightweight design make it an ideal companion for on-the-go productivity.

Verdict

The ASUS ExpertBook CX5403CMA-DB588 Chromebook Plus combines cutting-edge AI features, robust performance, a stunning display, and a durable build, making it a strong contender for professionals and students who rely on cloud-based tools. While details such as battery life could improve clarity, its integration of Google AI tools sets it apart from traditional Chromebooks.
