Acer Chromebook Spin 311 R721T-62ZQ Touchscreen Laptop

Brand: Acer
Operating System: Chrome OS
Display: 11.6" Touchscreen (1366x768)
Dimensions (WxDxH): 13.5" x 11.7" x 4.3"
Touch Screen: Yes
Weight: 1.0 lbs
Processor: 1.8 GHz AMD A6-9220C
Memory: 4 GB DDR4
Storage: 32 GB Flash Memory
WLAN: Wi-Fi
Colors: Black
Input Devices: Keyboard, Touchpad, Webcam, Touchscreen
Connectivity: Bluetooth, HDMI, USB, Wi-Fi

The Acer Chromebook Spin 311 R721T-62ZQ is a convertible 2-in-1 device designed for basic productivity and portability. At just 1.0 kg, this ultraportable chromebook boasts an 11.6-inch HD touchscreen display (1366 x 768 resolution), making it perfect for users who need flexibility without bulk.

Key Features:

  • Processor: The device comes equipped with an AMD A6-9220C processor. It operates with a base clock speed of 1.80 GHz, with the ability to boost up to 2.70 GHz, balancing power efficiency and performance for general use.
  • Memory and Storage: It includes 4 GB of DDR4 RAM and 32 GB of Flash Memory, suitable for lightweight tasks and storage of essential files.
  • Touch-Enabled: Its touchscreen display supports quick interaction, and its 2-in-1 design allows it to switch effortlessly between a laptop and tablet mode, tailored for productivity on the go.
  • Connectivity Options: The Chromebook offers robust connectivity, featuring Bluetooth, HDMI, USB, and Wi-Fi, ensuring compatibility with most peripherals and networks.
  • Build: Measuring a compact 11.7 x 13.5 x 4.3 inches, the Spin 311 is sturdy and portable, and its shale black finish gives it a sleek aesthetic.
  • Input Features: Along with a keyboard, touchpad, and webcam, it supports touch input, providing a versatile setup for varied user needs.

This Chromebook is designed to excel in versatility and simplicity, with Chrome OS offering a smooth, user-friendly experience. While its specs are tailored to basic workloads, its lightweight design and build quality make it particularly appealing to students, casual users, or anyone looking for a reliable device for everyday tasks.

If you're searching for a compact and functional Chromebook with a touch of flexibility, the Acer Spin 311 R721T-62ZQ might be worth considering.
