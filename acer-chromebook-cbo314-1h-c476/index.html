Acer Gateway Chromebook 314 CBO314-1H-C476 - Full HD Laptop

Brand: Acer
Operating System: ChromeOS
Display: 14" Full HD Widescreen ComfyView (1920x1080)
Dimensions (WxDxH): 12.85" x 9.0" x 0.74"
Weight: 3.31 lbs
Processor: 2.8 GHz Intel Celeron N4500
Memory: 4 GB LPDDR4X (max: 4 GB)
Storage: 64 GB eMMC
Battery life: 10.5 hours
WLAN: Dual-Band 2.4GHz and 5GHz
Colors: Black
Input Devices: Keyboard, Touchpad, Webcam
Connectivity: Bluetooth 5.0, HDMI, USB Type-C, USB 3.2 Gen 1, Wi-Fi 5 (802.11ac)

The Acer Gateway Chromebook 314 is a functional and budget-friendly device tailored for users seeking simple, efficient computing powered by ChromeOS. Below, we summarize its features and capabilities to give you a deeper insight into this machine.

Hardware and Display

This Chromebook is driven by an Intel Celeron N4500 dual-core processor with clock speeds up to 2.8GHz. Paired with 4GB of LPDDR4X RAM and 64GB eMMC storage, it's a solid choice for light productivity tasks, web browsing, and media consumption. The 14" Full HD (1920x1080) Widescreen ComfyView display offers sharp visuals, making it ideal for streaming and document editing.

Build and Design

Weighing just 3.31 pounds and measuring 12.85 x 9.0 x 0.74 inches (W x H x D), the Chromebook 314 is compact and portable. The Star Black finish ensures a minimalistic, professional look.

Connectivity and Ports

Connectivity includes Wi-Fi 5 (802.11ac) with 2x2 MU-MIMO technology for reliable wireless performance and Bluetooth 5.0 for peripherals. Expand your workspace with 1 USB Type-C port (USB 3.2 Gen 1, DisplayPort, USB charging), 1 USB 3.2 Gen 1 port, and a 3.5mm combo audio jack. Basic multimedia needs are addressed with two built-in stereo speakers and an HD webcam (720p).

Operating System and Ecosystem

ChromeOS powers the Gateway 314, offering seamless integration with Google's ecosystem, including Gmail, Google Drive, YouTube, and Play Store apps like Adobe Express and Microsoft 365 compatibility (subscription required). Features like auto-updates and the Titan C2 security chip ensure robust protection and secure browsing. The system boots in under 10 seconds, saving you valuable time.

Battery Life

This Chromebook delivers up to 10.5 hours of battery life, making it dependable for students and professionals needing all-day performance without constant recharging.

Summary

The Acer Gateway Chromebook 314 is a reliable choice for those who prioritize lightweight productivity in a secure environment. While it won't handle intensive workloads, its combination of Google-powered tools, thoughtful security, and efficiency makes it an excellent option for everyday tasks.
