Acer Chromebook 14 CB3-431-C0AK

Brand: Acer
Operating System: Chrome OS
Display: 14" Full HD IPS Display (1920x1080)
Dimensions (WxDxH): 13.4" x 9.3" x 0.7"
Weight: 3.42 lbs
Processor: 1.6 GHz Intel Celeron N3160
Memory: 4 GB LPDDR3
Storage: 32 GB eMMC
Battery life: 12 hours
WLAN: Wi-Fi 802.11 a/c
Colors: Luxury Gold
Input Devices: Keyboard, Touchpad, Microphone, Video Camera
Connectivity: USB

The Acer Chromebook 14 CB3-431-C0AK is a slim and lightweight laptop with a 14-inch full HD ComfyView Widescreen IPS display. It is equipped with a quad-core Intel Celeron N3160 processor, 4 GB RAM and 32 GB eMMC storage.

Moreover, this Chromebook has built-in 802.11AC Wi-Fi and Bluetooth 4.2, a HD webcam (1280 x 720) with 88 degree wide angle lens supporting High Dynamic Range (HDR), 2 x USB 3.0 ports, HDMI port and a 3-Cell Li-Polymer battery (3950 mAh) for up to 12 hours of battery life.

With its 100% Aluminum chassis in luxury gold the slim laptop ways a mere 3.42 lbs. Considering its weight and specs this Acer Chromebook is well suitable for mobile use and has enough power to get work done.

Some answers to questions that get asked frequently about this laptop:

  • the CB3-431-C0AK does not have a backlit keyboard
  • it has no SD card slot
  • it has no USB-C port for charging
  • the storage type is eMMC

The Acer CB3-431-C0AK was released in September 2016 and offered for an initial price of US $299.99.
